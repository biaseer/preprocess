Russia tightens grip on information about war in Ukraine
20220305
https://www.kcet.org/shows/newshour/clip/russian-invasion-ryan-1646434948
As Russia continues its assault on Ukraine, its increasingly using harsh tactics to control information about the war at home. Russia has banned Facebook, while also passing a law that could jail journalists and others for promulgating "fake news" about the military. This as oil bans against Russia are being considered internationally. Ryan Chilcote joins Judy Woodruff from Moscow to discuss.
