How Putins war is impacting ordinary Russians

Hundreds of Russians have been arrested for demonstrating against Ukraines invasion across the country. Meanwhile, sanctions aimed at crippling Russias economy are slowly having an impact: the ruble is fluctuating, people are queuing up outside ATMs and prices are rising. Anton Troianovski, Moscow Bureau Chief of the New York Times, joins.
