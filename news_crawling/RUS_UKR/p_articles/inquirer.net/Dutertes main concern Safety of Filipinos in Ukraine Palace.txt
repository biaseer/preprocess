MANILA, Philippines  The safety of Filipinos in Ukraine is the primary concern of President Rodrigo Duterte, acting presidential spokesman and Cabinet Secretary Karlo Nograles said on Thursday.
To date, six Filipinos have returned to the Philippines amid the crisis in Ukraine. Two more are expected to arrive.
The Philippine government, through the Department of Foreign Affairs, is now conducting repatriation efforts of Filipinos living in Ukraine, he added.FEATURED STORIES
The Overseas Workers Welfare Administration said that the government is prepared, should the need for mass repatriation arise.
