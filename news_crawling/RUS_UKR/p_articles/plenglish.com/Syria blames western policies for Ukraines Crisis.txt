Syria blames western policies for Ukraines Crisis
20220222
https://www.plenglish.com/news/2022/02/22/syria-blames-western-policies-for-ukraines-crisis/
Syrian Foreign Minister Faisal al-Mekdad on Tuesday announced his countrys support for Russian President Vladimir Putins decision to recognize the independence of the two Donbas republics.
During the reception of a Russian parliamentary delegation in December 2021, President Bashar al-Assad affirmed Syrias readiness to build and strengthen relations with the Luhansk and Donetsk republics, taking into account common interests and mutual respect, the text noted.
Damascus is convinced that the current problem is the result of the actions by the West, particularly the United States, aimed at dividing peoples and undermining Russias national security, the Syrian Presidency said in a statement.
It reiterated that confronting Western policies is a problem of mutual interest for all peoples opposing hegemony.
