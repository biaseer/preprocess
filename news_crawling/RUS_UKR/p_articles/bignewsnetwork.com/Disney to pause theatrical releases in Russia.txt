Washington US, March 1 ANI: Hollywood studio Disney has decided to pause its theatrical releases in Russia, following the country's military operation in Ukraine.
"Given the unprovoked invasion of Ukraine and the tragic humanitarian crisis, we are pausing the release of theatrical films in Russia," the statement read.
For the unversed, last week, Russian President Vladimir Putin announced military operation in Ukraine and warned other countries that any attempt to interfere with the Russian action would lead to "consequences they have never seen." ANI
