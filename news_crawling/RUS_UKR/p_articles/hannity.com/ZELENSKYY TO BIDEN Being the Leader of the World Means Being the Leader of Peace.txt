Im addressing the President Biden, Zelenskyy said. You, the leader of your nation, I wish you to be the leader of the world.
President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy made his case to members of the U.S. Congress Wednesday morning, asking once more for a no-fly zone over Ukraine. But if thats not possible, he wants weapons and fast.
Zelenskyy gave an impassioned speech, sharing a video of the destruction and loss Ukraine has suffered at the hand of Putin and the invading Russian army.
