Inverness rallies to show support for Ukraine after invasion by Russia
20220228
https://www.inverness-courier.co.uk/news/city-residents-show-support-for-ukraine-267430/
Inverness residents have shown solidarity with those caught up in the Ukraine conflict as well as providing practical support.
The owner of the A Touch of Poland store in Invernesss Tomnahurich Street, Tomasz Sawczuk, said it was accepting donations of all essential items.
Dozens of people turned out to a vigil held in Falcon Square in the city centre on Saturday as local businesses issued appeals for donations of goods to help in what is expected to become a major refugee crisis.
