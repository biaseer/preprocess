LVIV, Ukraine AP  Ukraines nuclear operator company said Thursday that Russian troops were leaving the Chernobyl nuclear power plant and were heading towards the countrys border with Belarus.
The operator, Energoatom said that the Russian military are also preparing to leave Slavutych, a nearby city where power plant workers live.
