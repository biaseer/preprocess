Russian withdrawals troops from Ukraine border
20211226
https://www.am1100theflag.com/news/national-news/40957-russian-withdrawals-troops-from-ukraine-border
Russia-- Russia is starting to withdraw some of its troops from the Ukraine border. That's according to Interfax news, which is reporting quotes from the Russian military. The troop withdrawal only seems to represent a fraction of the upwards of 100-thousand Russian troops thought to be stationed near the border.
