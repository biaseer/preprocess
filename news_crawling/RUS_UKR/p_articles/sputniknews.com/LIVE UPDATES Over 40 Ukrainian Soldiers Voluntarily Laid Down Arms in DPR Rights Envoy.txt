According to the Russian Ministry of Defence, Russia does not need the territory of Ukraine and it does not intend to stay in Ukraine, will withdraw its troops after the special operation is complete.
Russia announced a special military operation on 24 February with the goal of protection of the Donetsk and Lugansk People's Republics DPR and LPR from the Ukrainian forces, as well as demilitarization and denazification of Ukraine and bringing the puppet regime in Kiev to justice for the crimes it has committed. Moscow said the military operation does not pose a threat to civilians and only targets the military infrastructure.
