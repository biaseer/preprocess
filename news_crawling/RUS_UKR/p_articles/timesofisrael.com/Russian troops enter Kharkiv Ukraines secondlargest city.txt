Russian troops have reportedly entered Ukraines second-largest city  Kharkiv in the northeast of the country.
Videos circulating on social media appear to show a number of Russian light military vehicles on the streets of the city, with some footage showing vehicles on fire.
Hours earlier, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyys office said Russian forces blew up a gas pipeline in Kharkiv, prompting the government to warn people to protect themselves from the smoke by covering their windows with damp cloth or gauze.
