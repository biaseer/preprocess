Ukraine plant shelling could be much worse than Chernobyl
20220812
https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/av/world-europe-62526075
Speaking to the BBC, Ukraine's  ambassador to the International Atomic Energy Agency IAEA has warned about the consequences of missile strikes at Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant.
A blast at the plant would be "much worse than Chernobyl", Yevhenii Tsymbaliuk told BBC World Newss Tim Willcox.
The nuclear power plant has seen repeated shelling, with Ukraine and Russia blaming each other for the attack.
