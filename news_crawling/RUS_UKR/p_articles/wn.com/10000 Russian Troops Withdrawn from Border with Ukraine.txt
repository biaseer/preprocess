10000 Russian Troops Withdrawn from Border with Ukraine
20211228
https://article.wn.com/view/2021/12/28/10000_Russian_Troops_Withdrawn_from_Border_with_Ukraine/
10,000 Russian troops have been withdrawn from the border with Ukraine. Reuters reported the withdrawal on Saturday, pointing to Russian news agency Interfax as its source. Over the course of 2021, tensions between Russia and Ukraine have been escalating. As a result, an increasing number of Russian troops have been mobilised...
