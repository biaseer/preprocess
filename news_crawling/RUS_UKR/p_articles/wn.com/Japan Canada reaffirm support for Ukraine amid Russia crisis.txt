Japan Canada reaffirm support for Ukraine amid Russia crisis

The prime ministers of Japan and Canada on Wednesday expressed support for Ukraine amid heightened military tensions with Russia.  In a phone call, Prime Minister Fumio Kishida and his Canadian counterpart Justin Trudeau said their governments continue to monitor the situation in Ukraine with grave concern. ...
