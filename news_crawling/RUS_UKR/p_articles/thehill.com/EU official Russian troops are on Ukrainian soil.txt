Russian troops have entered in Donbas. We consider Donbas part of Ukraine, the EUs foreign policy chief, Josep Borrell, said, according to The Washington Post.
He later reportedly said that theEU plans to respond and will decide on sanctions against Moscowlater Tuesday.
A top European Union official said on Tuesday that Russian troops had entered breakaway territories in Ukraine.
In the past, the Kremlin has denied that it has placed troops in the breakaway territories, despite documentation from American, European and Ukrainian officials that have shown otherwise, thePostnoted.
Russian President Vladimir Putinhas labeled the move apeacekeeping effort, though the presence of Russian forces in Ukraine has been received by the international community as an escalation of the crisis.
I wouldnt say that it is a fully fledged invasion, but Russian troops are on Ukrainian soil, Borrell added.
Putin on Monday recognized two breakaway territories in eastern Ukraine as independent, marking a sharp escalation in tensions.
During a radio interview on Tuesday,Polish Defense Minister Mariusz Baszczak also said that Russian troops had violated international law by entering Ukraines territories, the newspaper reported.
Putin rejected diplomatic efforts to cease fighting and pledged more assistance to separatists battling Ukrainian forces in the Donetsk Peoples Republic and Luhansk Peoples Republic.
