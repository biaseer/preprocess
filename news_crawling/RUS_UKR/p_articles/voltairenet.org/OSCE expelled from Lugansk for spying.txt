OSCE expelled from Lugansk for spying
20220415
https://www.voltairenet.org/article216498.html
The Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe OSCE has replaced its staff with individuals who previously served as active officers in the NATO armies. They were granted diplomatic clearance by Kiev plus all the inherent protections.
Reports of espionage activities quickly emerged. Chairman of the Russian Federation Investigative Committee Alexander Bastrykin asked the Lugansk Peoples Republic to verify the allegations
