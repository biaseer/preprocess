THE PETROL and diesel prices in York are continuing to fluctuate as prices have slightly increased from last week.
The most expensive place to fuel in York is Knavesmire service station, Tadcaster Road, 166.8p for petrol and 174.8p for diesel -up from 167.8p for unleaded, but down from 182.9p for diesel atCo-op Boroughbridge Road last week.
The Press reported last week that the cheapest price for fuel was 154.7p for unleaded, down from 159.9p the week previously,and170.7p for diesel, down from 177.9p, at Morrisons, Foss Island Road.
Fuel prices hit a record high due to concerns over the reliability of supply amid the Russia-Ukraine conflict.
However this week, the cheapest price for fuel is 154.9p for petrol and 171.9p for diesel at Tesco, Tadcaster Road.
