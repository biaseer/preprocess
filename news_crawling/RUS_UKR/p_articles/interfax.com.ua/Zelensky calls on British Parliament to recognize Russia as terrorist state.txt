Zelensky calls on British Parliament to recognize Russia as terrorist state
20220308
https://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/809659.html
President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky called on the UK to recognize Russia as a "terrorist state". He announced this on Tuesday, speaking via video link in the British House of Commons.
"Strengthen sanctions against the terrorist state. Recognize it Russia a terrorist state. Find a way to make our Ukrainian sky safe. Do what you can. What you should. What does the greatness of your state and your people oblige you to do?" he said.
