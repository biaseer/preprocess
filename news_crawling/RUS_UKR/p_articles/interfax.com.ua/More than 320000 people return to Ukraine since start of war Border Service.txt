More than 320000 people return to Ukraine since start of war  Border Service
20220317
https://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/815058.html
Since the beginning of the open armed aggression of the Russian Federation against Ukraine, more than 320,000 Ukrainians, most of them men, have returned to their homeland, the State Border Guard Service of Ukraine reports on its Telegram channel.
"Our guys do not give up, so we need to help, we need to fight for our country. Ukraine must be free, like all people," one of the Ukrainians who arrived from Poland says.
