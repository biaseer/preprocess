Russia Ukraine War Over 200 Nigerians apply to join Ukrainian Army
20220303
https://www.thenews-chronicle.com/russia-ukraine-war-over-200-nigerians-apply-to-join-ukrainian-army/
According to an anonymous source the government of Ukraine has shortlisted about 100 who are most qualified as the government is keen on recruiting more qualified men such as ex -service men and with military background .
Over 200 Nigerians have indicated their interest to join Ukraine in defending her home from the Russian invasion. According to the reports, the number of Nigerian volunteers is increasing and is likely to hit 400 before the end of the week.
