Ukraine says Russian troop buildup insufficient for major attack
20220126
https://www.arabnews.com/node/2012306/world
KIEV: Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba said Wednesday that the number of Russian troops deployed along his countrys border was not enough for a major attack.The number of Russian troops amassed along the border of Ukraine and occupied territories of Ukraine is large, it poses a threat to Ukraine, a direct threat to Ukraine, Kuleba told reporters.However, at the moment, as we speak, this number is insufficient for a full-scale offensive against Ukraine along the entire Ukrainian border.
