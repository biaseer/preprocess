On Saturday, three Russian helicopters were shot down by Ukrainian forces in the Mykolaiv region in the southern part of the country.
By India Today Web Desk: The Ministry of Defence of Ukraine has posted a tweet claiming air defence specialists have shot down a Russian attack aircraft. The incident took place on the outskirts of Chernihiv, a city in the northern part of Ukraine, on Saturday, March 5.
The Ukrainian Air Force said that five Russian aircraft and 4 helicopters were downed on Saturday, March 5. Ukraine claims a total of 44 Russian planes and helicopters have been brought down.
