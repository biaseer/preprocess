The photos you see are possibly the last lifetime photos of the Moskva cruiser, taken by the satellite company Maxar at occupied Sevastopol and near Snake Island in April this year.
According to unconfirmed reports, a Bayraktar TB2 UAV was sent to distract the crew's attention, after which a missile strike was launched from near Odessa.
Russian military blogs are spreading information that the attack by Ukraine on the Russian Black Sea Fleet cruiser Moskva was complex, the UNIAN news agency has reported.
