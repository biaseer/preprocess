AFU Launched Seven Powerful Strikes Against Enemy Groups
20220316
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/3/16/459305/
"Our troops carried out minor tactical counterattacks in some directions yesterday, which improved the situation in specific defence areas. Our troops also carried out powerful missile and artillery and air strikes against enemy groups. At least seven such strikes were carried out during the day. The air force shot down three aircraft, one helicopter and several cruise missiles," Arestovych said.
Yesterday the Ukrainian Armed Forces launched a counter-offensive in several directions. The Ukrainian military managed to inflict 7 powerful strikes against the groups of Russian occupiers.
The Ukrainian army's tactical offensive has improved its position in several sections of the front.
This was reported by RBC-Ukraine with reference to a briefing by the adviser to the head of the Office of the President Oleksiy Arestovych.
