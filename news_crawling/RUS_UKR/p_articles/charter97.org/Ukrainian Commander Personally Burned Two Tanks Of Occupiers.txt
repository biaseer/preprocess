In the photo, Afghan is holding an anti-tank missile launcher on his shoulder. Apparently, this launcher helped him to destroy enemy vehicles near Chernihiv. The General Staff does not disclose the time of these events in the message. "Be like Hryhoriy," they just add.

Hryhoriy with the call sign Afghan commands one of the Armed Forces of Ukraine brigades, the General Staff reported.
