Russian Troops Shell Smolensk Mother Of God Icon Church in Kharkiv
20220317
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/3/17/459457/
On the night of March 17, the Russian invaders once again shelled Kharkiv. By these actions, the Russians caused damage not only to residential buildings, but also to the Smolensk Mother of God Icon Church on Kholodna Mountain, informs the Kharkiv diocese of the Ukrainian Orthodox Church.
Fortunately, there were no injuries or deaths. This was also reported in the Kharkiv diocese of the Ukrainian Orthodox Church.
However, the religious building itself was damaged: the roof was destroyed, windows and doors were broken, and church utensils were damaged.
