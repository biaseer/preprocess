Ukrainian Artillerymen Destroy Two Enemy Akatsiya SelfPropelled Artillery Mounts In Donetsk Region
20220805
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/8/5/509988/
In the Donetsk region, Ukrainian artillerymen destroyed two Akatsiya self-propelled artillery mounts of the Russian army.
Our beautiful Ukrainian land in the Donetsk region, from which two self-propelled artillery mounts 2S3 Akatsiya were successfully burned out. The work was carried out by the 43rd Separate Artillery Brigade named after Hetman Taras Tryasilo and our brothers-in-arms from the 26th Artillery Brigade named after Cornet General Roman Dashkevich.
