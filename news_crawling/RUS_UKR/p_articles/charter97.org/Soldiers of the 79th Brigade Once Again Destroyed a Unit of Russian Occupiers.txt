Soldiers of the 79th Brigade Once Again Destroyed a Unit of Russian Occupiers
20220503
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/5/3/466064/
Soldiers of the 79th Separate Airborne Assault Brigade of the Airborne Assault Troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine again destroyed a unit of Russian occupiers.
It should be noted that the units of the Mykolaiv Brigade, together with other military units of the AFU Airborne Assault Troops continue to destroy the forces of the Russian occupiers in all major directions," the report reads.
Among the personal belongings of the destroyed occupiers was an e-ticket for the Ufa-Chebarkul flight.
