Occupants Show In Belarusian Mozyr How They Prepared To Assault Gostomel
20220716
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/7/16/506900/
A Russian propaganda telegram channel posted a video of Russian troops heading to storm Gostomel in the Kyiv region early on February 24.
It is claimed that the video shows the 45th Separate Guards Brigade of the Russian Airborne Troops Special Forces. The loading of the troops took place at the airfield in Mozyr.
The video shows Russian troops loading into trucks and helicopters marked with V. The flight, landing and assault of Antonov airport in Gostomel are shown.
