Ukrainian Military Published Video of New Captive Invaders
20220225
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/2/25/456214/
The Armed Forces of Ukraine have published a video of a group of captured Russian servicemen, pravda.com.ua. reported.
The Army also published another video with a Russian soldier who surrendered in the center of Sumy.
The video shows four enemy soldiers. One of them says they were driving behind a column and lost it before the city.
