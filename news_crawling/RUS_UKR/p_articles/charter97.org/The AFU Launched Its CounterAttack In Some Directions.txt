The AFU Launched Its CounterAttack In Some Directions
20220315
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/3/15/459163/
Valeri Zaluzhnyi, the Commander-In-Chief of Armed forces of Ukraine, summed up 20 days of battles of the Ukrainian fighters with the Russian occupiers, UNIAN reports.
The Commander-In-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine also mentioned losses of the Ukrainian military.
Zaluzhnyi also added that the Ukrainian servicemen suffered losses. But they are several times smaller than the opponent's. He expressed his condolences to the families of the dead servicemen.
"We stopped the aggressor, deployed parts of our reserve and we are launching counter-offensive in certain directions," he writes.
