Ukrainian Armed Forces Mortar Troopers Destroy Enemy Column With Accurate Shots

This is reported by the press service of the 30th separate mechanized brigade named after Konstantin Ostrozhsky.
"Snow is no obstacle for our mortar men. It takes the enemy to come within firing range. The mortar men of the 30th separate mechanized brigade eloquently prove their class by accurate shooting. This is how the duty column of enemy equipment has been destroyed," the brigade captioned the video.
