Ukrainians Blocked Russian Convoys Attempts To Advance Towards Dnipro From Balakliia
20220306
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/3/6/457797/
This is stated in the summary of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine as of 6 a.m., March 6, 2022, nv.ua reported.
"The enemy tries not to lose offensive potential, but in the current situation has large losses of weapons, equipment and personnel. The enemy is demoralized due to the resistance of entire Ukrainian people, the General Staff added.
Subdivisions of the Armed Forces of Ukraine blocked convoys of the Russian occupiers trying to advance towards the Dnipropetrovsk region from Balakliia.
