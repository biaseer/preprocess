Locksmiths Teachers Social Workers Kremlin Throws Them Into Fray To Die
20220302
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/3/2/457093/
People in Donetsk and Luhansk regions have been forced to take weapons by deception and blackmail.
The Headquarters of the United Forces Operation reported that Ukrainian defenders had captured a group of men who had been fighting on Russia's side in the Donetsk direction. As it turned out, there were not a single military man among them: locksmiths, teachers, social workers. They were deceived or blackmailed to go to the military enlistment office and fight against Ukraine. The command has posted a corresponding video on Facebook, zn.ua reported.
