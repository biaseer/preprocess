Azov Fighters Destroy Command Post Arsenal Of Russian Troops
20220513
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/5/13/467574/
The Azov fighters published the video of the strike on Friday, May 13, on their Telegram channe.l.
Ukrainian defenders from the Dnieper territorial defense unit Azov destroyed the command post of the Russian troops.
Following the movements of the invaders' supply trucks, they discovered a building that the invaders used as a temporary command post and ammunition depot, after which a powerful blow was dealt to it.
