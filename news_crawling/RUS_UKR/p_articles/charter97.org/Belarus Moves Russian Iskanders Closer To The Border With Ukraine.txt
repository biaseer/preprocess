Belarus Moves Russian Iskanders Closer To The Border With Ukraine
20220522
https://charter97.org/en/news/2022/5/22/468969/
Despite the fact that the Russian Federation retreated from the Kyiv direction, Belarus continues to retain part of the Russian equipment and personnel of the occupier on its territories. Separate missile launches from the country are still being recorded.
Today, for the first time in a long time, the monitoring group recorded the movement of Iskanders to the border with Ukraine.
Belarus deploys Russian Iskanders closer to the border with Ukraine. This happened for the first time in two months of the war in Ukraine.
