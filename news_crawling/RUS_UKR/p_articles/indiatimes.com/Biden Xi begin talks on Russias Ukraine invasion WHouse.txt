US President Joe Biden and Chinese leader Xi Jinping began a phone call Friday in which Biden will pressure his counterpart to join Western pressure on Russia over the invasion of Ukraine. The two leaders started the call, their first since a video summit in November, at 9:03 am 1303 GMT, the White House said.
