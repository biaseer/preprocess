Why RussiaUkraine talks could this time yield results

As Russia-Ukraine diplomats meet today in Turkey, anyone hoping for a peaceful solution must understand the complicated history, which will point to possible resolutions of the crisis. President Vladimir Putin insists Ukraines potential membership of the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation Nato is an existential threat to Russia. Commentators in the West have focussed on Putins aversion to democracy and his desire to crush the experiment in Ukraine lest it becomes an example for Russia. In a televised speech just before attacking Ukraine, Putin revealed his deeply held belief that Ukraine had no right to be an independent country and blamed Lenin for granting Ukraine the status of a Republic.
