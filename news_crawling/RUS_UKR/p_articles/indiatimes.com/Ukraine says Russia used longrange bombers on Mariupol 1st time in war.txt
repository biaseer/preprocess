Ukraine says Russia used longrange bombers on Mariupol 1st time in war

https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/world/europe/ukraine-says-russia-used-long-range-bombers-on-mariupol-1st-time-in-war/articleshow/90865089.cms
Ukraine's defence ministry spokesman Oleksandr Motuzyanyk said on Friday that for, the first time since the start of its invasion, Russia used long-range bombers to attack the besieged port city of Mariupol.Motuzyanyk said Russia was concentrating its efforts on seizing the cities of Rubizhne, Popasna and Mariupol.
