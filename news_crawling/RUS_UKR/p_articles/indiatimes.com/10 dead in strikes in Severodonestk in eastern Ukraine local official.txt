10 dead in strikes in Severodonestk in eastern Ukraine local official
20220309
https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/international/world-news/10-dead-in-strikes-in-severodonestk-in-eastern-ukraine-local-official/articleshow/90098920.cms
At least 10 people were killed in a Russian military attack in the eastern Ukrainian town of Severodonestk on Tuesday, a local official for the Lugansk region said in a statement on Telegram.The Russian military "opened fire" on residential homes and other buildings in the town, he said, without immediately specifying whether it was an artillery attack.The region has seen heavy fighting in recent days.
