Russian officials had told Ankara that Russia had "nothing to do" with the attacks on Ukraine's key Black Sea port of Odesa, Turkish Defence Minister Hulusi Akar has said."In our contact with Russia, the Russians told us that they had absolutely nothing to do with these attacks and that they were examining the issue very closely and in detail," Akar told Turkiye's state-run Anadolu Agency."The fact that such an incident happened right after the agreement we made yesterday regarding the grain shipment worried us," he said.Akar said he also had phone conversations with Ukrainian ministers and received information regarding the incident, Xinhua news agency reported.The Ukrainian military said that Russian missiles hit infrastructure in Odesa on Saturday.
