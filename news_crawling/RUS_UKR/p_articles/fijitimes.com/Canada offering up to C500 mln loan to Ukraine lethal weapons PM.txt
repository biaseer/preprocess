The goal of this support from Canada and our other partners is to dissuade Russia from pursuing its aggression towards Ukraine, Prime Minister Justin Trudeau told reporters.
OTTAWA, Feb 14 Reuters  Canada will offer a loan of up to C$500 million $393 million to support Ukraine, which is bracing for a possible Russian invasion, and also said it would provide C$7.8 mln worth of lethal equipment and ammunition, the prime minister said on Monday.
