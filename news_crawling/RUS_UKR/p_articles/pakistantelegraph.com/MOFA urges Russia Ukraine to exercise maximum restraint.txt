KATHMANDU, Nepal - The Nepal government has expressed concern about the recent Russia-Ukraine crisis, opposing Russia's attack on its neighbor.
According to a statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on Thursday, Nepal "calls on all parties concerned to exercise maximum restraint to avoid further escalated tension."
"In any circumstance, Nepal opposes the use of force against a sovereign country and believes in the peaceful resolution of disputes through diplomacy and dialogue."
