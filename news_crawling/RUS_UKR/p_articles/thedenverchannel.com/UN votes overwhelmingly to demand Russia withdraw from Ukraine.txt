UNITED NATIONS  The U.N. General Assembly has voted to demand that Russia stop its offensive in Ukraine and withdraw all troops, with nations from world powers to tiny island states condemning Moscow.
Countries that spoke up for Russia included Belarus, North Korea and Syria. Cuba also spoke in support of Russia but did not issue a vote.
The resolution deplored Russias aggression against Ukraine in the strongest terms and demanded an immediate halt to Moscows use of force and the immediate and complete withdrawal of all Russian forces.
