Russian missiles hit Kramatorsk
20220720
https://www.dw.com/en/russian-missiles-strike-kramatorsk-in-push-to-take-donbas/av-62534691
Russian missiles struck a residential area of Kramatorsk, a city in eastern Ukraine Tuesday. At least one person died and several others were wounded. Russian troops are increasingly targeting the city as they try push further into the Donbas region.
