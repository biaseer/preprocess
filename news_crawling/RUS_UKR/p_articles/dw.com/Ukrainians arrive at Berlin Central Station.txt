Ukrainians arrive at Berlin Central Station

Since the Russian invasion of Ukraine, people fleeing the war have been arriving at Berlin Central Station. Every day, thousands arrive on trains. Many local volunteers help out to make sure new arrivals fleeing the war can seek help.
