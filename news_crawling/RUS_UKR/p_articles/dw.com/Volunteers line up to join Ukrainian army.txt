Volunteers line up to join Ukrainian army
20220306
https://www.dw.com/en/volunteers-line-up-in-kyiv-to-join-ukrainian-army/av-61032203
Despite Russia's increasingly brutal tactics in its war against Ukraine, volunteers are still signing up to join Ukraine's army. Men aged between 18 and 60 have been told they must remain in the country so that they are available for conscription.
