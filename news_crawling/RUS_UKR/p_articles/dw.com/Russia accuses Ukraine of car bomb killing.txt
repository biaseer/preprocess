Russia accuses Ukraine of car bomb killing

Russia has accused Ukraine of carrying out a car bomb attack that killed Darya Dugina, the daughter of a Putin ally. Kyiv denies any involvement. The allegation comes amid speculation within Ukraine that a group inside Russia could have been responsible.
