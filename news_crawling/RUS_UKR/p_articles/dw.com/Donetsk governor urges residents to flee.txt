Donetsk governor urges  residents to flee

Ukrainian officials have urged some 350,000 residents of the eastern Donetsk region to evacuate. The Donetsk governor says there is no safe place, as Russian forces step up attacks. On Tuesday Russian missiles struck a market in the city of Slovyansk.
