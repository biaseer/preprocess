Ukraine digs in for Russian assault on Donetsk
20220706
https://www.dw.com/en/ukraine-digs-in-for-russian-assault-on-donetsk/av-62388931
Ukraine defense forces are preparing for a ground assault by Russian troops in the Donetsk industrial region in eastern Ukraine. The local governor says there is no safe place left and is urging the remaining 350,000 residents to leave.
