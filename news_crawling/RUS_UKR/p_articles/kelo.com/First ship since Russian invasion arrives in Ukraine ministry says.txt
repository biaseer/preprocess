Bulk carrier FULMAR S has arrived at Chornomorsk port and is ready for loading, the ministry said on Facebook.
KYIV Reuters      The first cargo vessel since the Russian invasion arrived at Ukrainian Black Sea port of Chornomorsk for the future transportation of grain to international markets, Ukraines Infrastructure Ministry said on Sunday.
Earlier on Sunday, four ships carrying Ukrainian foodstuffs sailed from Ukrainian Black Sea ports as part of a deal to unblock the countrys sea exports.
