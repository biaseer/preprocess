Ukraine has filed more eight war crimes cases to court  prosecutor
20220608
https://kelo.com/2022/06/08/ukraine-has-filed-more-eight-war-crimes-cases-to-court-prosecutor/
KYIV Reuters      Ukraine has filed eight more war crimes cases to court in addition to three sentences already handed down to Russian soldiers, Ukraines Prosecutor General Iryna Venediktova said on Wednesday.
Ukraine has opened more than 16,000 investigations into possible war crimes during Russias invasion which began on Feb. 24, she said on television.
