Reuters      Russian forces pushed closer to the centre of Ukraines Sieverodonetsk as the fighting intensified over May 30-31, Britains defence ministry said on Wednesday.
Over half of the town is likely now occupied by Russian forces, including Chechen fighters, the ministry said in an intelligence update posted on Twitter.
