Russia MOD releases footage of use of its Ka52 Alligator helicopters in Ukraine
20220312
https://news.am/eng/news/691034.html
It is stated that guided and unguided missiles were used in order to destroy the facilities of the Ukrainian army.
These helicopters have destroyed the armored vehicles and convoy of vehicles of the Ukrainian armed forces, the Russian defense ministry said.
The Ministry of Defense MOD Russia has released the footage of the use of its Ka-52 "Alligator" helicopters in Ukraine, Interfax-AVN reports.
