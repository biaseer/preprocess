Russia Deputy FM does not rule out possibility of discussing exchange of POWs from Azovstal
20220523
https://news.am/eng/news/703359.html
"I allow any possibility that does not contradict common sense," he said Monday when asked by reporters whether Moscow allows the possibility of discussing this exchange.
Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Andrey Rudenko did not rule out the possibility of discussing the exchange of Russian prisoners for Ukrainian military from Azovstal, if it does not contradict common sense, TASS reported.
