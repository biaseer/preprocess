Ukraine army general staff denies information about Russia troops landing in Odessa
20220224
https://news.am/eng/news/688342.html
"Information about the landing of Russian troops in Odessa does not correspond to reality," the statement added.
The General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine reported that Russian troops fired missiles and dropped bombs at six Ukrainian airports. The respective statement was posted on the Facebook page of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
