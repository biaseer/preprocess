Kremlin not considering CSTO forces engagement in Ukraine
20220620
https://news.am/eng/news/708131.html
The option of engaging Collective Security Treaty Organization CSTO forces in the special operation in Ukraine is not considered, Russian presidential spokesman Dmitry Peskov told reporters Monday, according to RIA Novosti.
"No, that option is not being considered," Peskov told reporters when asked if the Kremlin was considering the option of engaging CSTO forces in the Russian special operation in Ukraine.
