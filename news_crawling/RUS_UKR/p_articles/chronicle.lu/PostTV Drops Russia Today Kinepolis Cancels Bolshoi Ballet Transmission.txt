PostTV Drops Russia Today Kinepolis Cancels Bolshoi Ballet Transmission
20220228
https://chronicle.lu/category/at-home/39822-posttv-drops-russia-today-kinepolis-cancels-bolshoi-ballet-transmission
Kinepolis announced cancelation of a screening of Swan Lake by the Bolshoi Ballet from its programming at Cin Utopia.
The decision followed after the European Commission's announcement to ban the broadcasting of certain Russian television channels within the European Union, and after consultation with the competent national authorities.
On Monday 28 February 2022, POST Luxembourg has announced that it has canceled the "RT - Russia Today" channel from its "PostTV" television offers, effective immediately.
