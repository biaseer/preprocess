ELMAU, Germany AP  The Group of Seven economic powers are set to announce an agreement to pursue a price cap on Russian oil, aiming to curb Moscows energy revenues, a U.S. official said Monday. The move is part of a joint effort of support for Ukraine that includes raising tariffs on Russian goods and imposing new sanctions on hundreds of Russian officials and entities supporting the four month long war.
