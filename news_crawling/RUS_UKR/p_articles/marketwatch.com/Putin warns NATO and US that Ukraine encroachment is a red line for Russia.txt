Putin warns NATO and US that Ukraine encroachment is a red line for Russia

https://www.marketwatch.com/story/putin-warns-nato-and-u-s-that-ukraine-encroachment-is-a-red-line-for-russia-01638296934
MOSCOW AP  Russian President Vladimir Putin on Tuesday sternly warned NATO against deploying its troops and weapons to Ukraine, saying it represents a red line for Russia and would trigger a strong response.
Commenting on Western concerns about Russias alleged intention to invade Ukraine, he said that Moscow is equally worried about NATO drills near its borders.
