Fleeing to the border Some 120000 Ukrainians seek refuge with Poland and other neighbors

https://www.marketwatch.com/story/fleeing-to-the-border-some-120-000-ukrainians-seek-refuge-with-poland-and-other-neighbors-01645894049
Some walked many miles through the night while others fled by train, car or bus, forming lines miles long at border crossings. They were greeted by waiting relatives and friends or headed on their own to reception centers organized by governments.
Tens of thousands of Ukrainians rushed to the borders as invading Russian troops pressed their advance into Ukraine and toward the countrys capital Saturday in Europes largest ground war since World War II.
