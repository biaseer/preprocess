Foreign Office prioritising humanitarian aid as Putin wields famine threat
20220621
https://www.politics.co.uk/parliament/foreign-office-prioritising-humanitarian-aid-as-putin-wields-famine-threat/
The minister told MPs that his department will prioritise humanitarian response in future funding for our department.
Catherine McKinnel asked foreign minister James Cleverly about what the government can do to address the grain shortage resulting from the Ukraine war.
Cleverly told the House of Commons that it is clear that Vladimir Putin is using hunger in the global South as a weapon of war.
