Ottawa Canada, February 24 ANI: Condemning Russia's military operation in eastern Ukraine, Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau on Thursday called on Moscow to withdraw all military from the country.
"Putin has just launched a full-scale invasion of Ukraine. Peaceful Ukrainian cities are under strikes. This is a war of aggression. Ukraine will defend itself and will win. The world can and must stop Putin. The time to act is now," Kuleba tweeted.
After Russian President Vladimir Putin announced a special "military operation" in the Donbas region, the Ukrainian ambassador to the United Nations requested the members of the UN to "stop the war."At an emergency meeting of the Security Council, Ukrainian envoy Sergiy Kyslytsya said, "It is the responsibility of this body to stop the war. So I call on every one of you to do everything possible to stop the war."Kyslytsya added it was "too late" for de-escalation and urged other countries to help stop the conflict.
