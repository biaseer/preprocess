LONDON Reuters      Britains biggest domestic lender Lloyds said on Thursday it was on heightened alert for cyberattacks from Russia as the crisis in Ukraine has worsened.
Russian forces fired missiles at several cities in Ukraine and landed troops on its coast on Thursday.
Weve been on heightened alert internally around our cyber risk controls and weve been focused on this for quite a while, Lloyds CEO Charlie Nunn told reporters after the banks full-year results.
