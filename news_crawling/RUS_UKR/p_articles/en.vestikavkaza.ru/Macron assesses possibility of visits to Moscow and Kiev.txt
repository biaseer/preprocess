Macron assesses possibility of visits to Moscow and Kiev
20220315
https://en.vestikavkaza.ru/news/Macron-assesses-possibility-of-visits-to-Moscow-and-Kiev.html
French President Emmanuel Macron said that he was not going to visit Moscow and Kiev in the near future.
At the same time, the head of the French state admitted that they could take place in the future. "I do not exclude anything, no political initiative. I will do it when I understand that there can be useful and tangible results",RIA Novosti quoted him.
According to Macron, the tripsare not expected either in the coming days or in the coming hours.
