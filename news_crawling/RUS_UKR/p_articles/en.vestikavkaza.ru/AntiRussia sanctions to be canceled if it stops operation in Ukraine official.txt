New sanctions of the West against Moscow will be canceled in case Russia halts its special operation in Ukraine, Under Secretary of State for Political Affairs Victoria Nuland told TASS in an interview.
"The sanctions will end if he Russian President Vladimir Putin - TASS ends this war, and helps rebuild Ukraine and reestablishes peace and recognizes the country's sovereignty and territorial integrity and right to exist," Nuland said.
