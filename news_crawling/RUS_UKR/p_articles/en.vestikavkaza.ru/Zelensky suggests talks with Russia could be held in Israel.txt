Zelensky suggests talks with Russia could be held in Israel
20220321
https://en.vestikavkaza.ru/news/Zelensky-suggests-talks-with-Russia-could-be-held-in-Israel.html
Ukrainian PresidentVolodymyr Zelensky suggested that Israelcould be the meeting ground forfuture peace talks with Russia,Reuters reports.
"We are grateful for his efforts, so that sooner or later we will begin to have talks with Russia, possibly in Jerusalem," he said. "That's the right place to find peace. If possible."
In his daily video update to Ukrainian citizens, Zelensky said Israel's Prime MinisterNaftali Bennett was making concerted efforts to negotiate peace talks between the two countries.
