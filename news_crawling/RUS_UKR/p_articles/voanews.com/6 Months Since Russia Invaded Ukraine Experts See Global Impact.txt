As Ukraine marks the six-month anniversary of Russias invasion, VOAs Senior Diplomatic Correspondent Cindy Saine reports on the fallout from the conflict. Millions of refugees have fled the country, food exports have plummeted and the United Nations is warning that the world faces maximum danger over the situation at Ukraines Zaporizhzhya nuclear power plant.
