International Volunteers in the Ukrainian Army Coming and Going
20220323
https://www.voanews.com/a/international-volunteers-in-the-ukrainian-army-coming-and-going/6497569.html
Thousands of international volunteers have come to Ukraine to help in the fight against Russias invasion. Many are former soldiers and their reasons for coming to Ukraine vary. As VOAs Celia Mendoza reports from Korczowa, Poland, some are now going back home.
