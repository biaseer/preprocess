Over 217 mln refugees enter Poland since war in Ukraine started
20220323
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/over-217-mln-refugees-enter-poland-since-war-in-ukraine-started-29018
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2,175,000 people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Wednesday morning.
The SG wrote on Twitter that on Tuesday Border Guard officers carried out 31,000 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine.
The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Wednesday reached 5,900 people.
