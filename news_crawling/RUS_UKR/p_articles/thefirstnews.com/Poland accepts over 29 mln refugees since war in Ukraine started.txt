Poland accepts over 29 mln refugees since war in Ukraine started
20220423
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/poland-accepts-over-29-mln-refugees-since-war-in-ukraine-started-29867
According to the SG, on Friday, Border Guard officers carried out around 17,700 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, a drop of 11 percent compared to the previous day.
The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Saturday had reached 4,000 people.
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, a total of 2,922,000 people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG said on Saturday morning.
