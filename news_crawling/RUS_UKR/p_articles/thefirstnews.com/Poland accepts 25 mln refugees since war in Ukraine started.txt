Poland accepts 25 mln refugees since war in Ukraine started
20220405
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/poland-accepts-25-mln-refugees-since-war-in-ukraine-started-29416
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2.5 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Tuesday morning.
The SG wrote on Twitter that on Monday, Border Guard officers carried out 18,500 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, a decrease of 17 percent compared to the previous day.
The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Tuesday had reached 3,800 people.
