The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Saturday had reached 5,400 people.
The SG wrote on Twitter that, on Friday, Border Guard officers carried out 24,400 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, a drop of 9 percent compared to the previous day.
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2.781 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Saturday morning.
