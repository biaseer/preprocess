Poland accepts nearly 24 mln refugees since war in Ukraine started
20220331
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/poland-accepts-nearly-24-mln-refugees-since-war-in-ukraine-started-29267
The SG wrote on Twitter that on Wednesday Border Guard officers carried out 25,500 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, an increase of 14 percent compared to the previous day.
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2.393 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Thursday morning.
The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Thursday had reached 4,500 people.
