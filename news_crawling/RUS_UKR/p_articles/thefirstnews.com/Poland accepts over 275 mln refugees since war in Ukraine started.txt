The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Friday had reached 6,200 people.
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2.757 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Friday morning.
The SG wrote on Twitter that, on Thursday, Border Guard officers carried out 26,800 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, a growth of 8 percent compared to the previous day.
