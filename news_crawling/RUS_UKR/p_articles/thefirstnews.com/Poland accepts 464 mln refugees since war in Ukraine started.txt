Poland accepts 464 mln refugees since war in Ukraine started
20220709
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/poland-accepts-464-mln-refugees-since-war-in-ukraine-started-31630
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 4.64 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Saturday morning.
According to the SG, on Friday, Border Guard officers carried out around 23,100 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, and by 7:00 a.m. on Saturday the number of arrivals had reached around 7,500 people.
The Border Guard also said that 2.71 million people had left Poland for Ukraine since the beginning of the war. On Friday, 22,500 people crossed the border from Poland to Ukraine.
