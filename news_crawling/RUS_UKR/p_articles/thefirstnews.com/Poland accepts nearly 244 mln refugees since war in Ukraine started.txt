Poland accepts nearly 244 mln refugees since war in Ukraine started
20220402
https://www.thefirstnews.com/article/poland-accepts-nearly-244-mln-refugees-since-war-in-ukraine-started-29357
Since February 24, when Russia invaded Ukraine, 2.437 million people have crossed the Polish-Ukrainian border into Poland, the Border Guard SG tweeted on Saturday morning.
The Border Guard also said that the number of arrivals by 7:00 a.m. on Saturday had reached 4,300 people, up by 11 percent compared to Friday.
The SG wrote on Twitter that on Friday Border Guard officers carried out 21,100 border checks on people entering Poland from Ukraine, a drop of 8.3 percent compared to the previous day.
