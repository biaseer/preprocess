People are invited to gather in the city for a silent vigil to mourn all those who have died in the war. There will also be videos shown about the war in Ukraine to raise awareness about its global consequences.
An organiser of the event said: "We need more sanctions against Russia, Belarus and the oligarchs, and the provision of more weapons for Ukraines defenders.
"We urge Home Secretary Priti Patel to ditch the visa rules for refugees from Ukraine."
A candlelight vigil will be held in Cambridge tonight March 10 to show solidarity with Ukraine.

Today marks two weeks since Russia invaded Ukraine. The vigil will be held by the Cambridge University Ukrainian Society in King's Parade, where a number of their protests have been held.
The Cambridge University Ukrainian society has been protesting every day for the past 14 days.
