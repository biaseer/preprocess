PARIS  French Foreign Affairs Minister Jean-Yves Le Drian said on Sunday that he firmly condemned what he called the massive abuses committed by Russian forces in Ukraine in the recent weeks.
Le Drian mentioned in particular the town of Bucha outside Kyiv, where Ukrainian authorities say a deliberate massacre was carried out by Russia.
