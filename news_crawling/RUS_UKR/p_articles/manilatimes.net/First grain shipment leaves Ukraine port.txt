The blockage of deliveries from warring Russia and Ukraine  two of the world's biggest grain exporters  has contributed to soaring food prices, hitting the world's poorest nations especially hard.
KYIV: The first shipment of Ukrainian grain left the Black Sea port of Odesa on Monday morning, Turkey announced, as Kyiv said Moscow's "brutal" shelling of the southern city of Mykolaiv had killed an agriculture tycoon.
