RussiaUkraine war 1306 Ukrainian citizens arrive in Israel 18 turned away
20220317
https://www.msn.com/en-us/news/world/russia-ukraine-war-1-306-ukrainian-citizens-arrive-in-israel-18-turned-away/ar-AAVaRim
Over the last 24 hours, 1,306 Ukrainian citizens arrived at Israel's Ben-Gurion Airport, the Aliyah and Integration Ministry announced, according to N12. Of them, 18 were refused entry. Since the beginning of Russia's invasion of Ukraine on February 24, a total of 11,390 Ukrainian citizens arrived in Israel, of which 258 were refused entry.
