Ahead of the summit, Biden said Ukraine could join NATO if they meet the criteria, reports The New York Times.
Ahead of the meeting between Biden and Putin, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky announced that his government would reject any agreement that the U.S. and Russia reached without Ukraines input, says The New York Times.
