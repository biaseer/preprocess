Ukraine seeks longterm solutions to help it win war with Russia
20220407
https://wtbx.com/2022/04/07/ukraine-seeks-long-term-solutions-to-help-it-win-war-with-russia/
Reuters      Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba, who was holding talks with G7 and NATO nations on Thursday, said his country was seeking long-term solutions to help it win its war with Russia.
I came here today to discuss three most important things: weapons, weapons, and weapons. Ukraines urgent needs, the sustainability of supplies, and long-term solutions which will help Ukraine to prevail, Kuleba wrote in a tweet which he said was sent from NATO headquarters in Brussels.
