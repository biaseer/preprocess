Russia says checkpoint in Kursk region shelled from Ukraine
20220430
https://wtbx.com/2022/04/30/russia-says-checkpoint-in-kursk-region-shelled-from-ukraine/
Reuters      The governor of Russias western Kursk region said several shells were fired on Saturday at a checkpoint near its border from the direction of Ukraine.
Speaking in a video posted on his Telegram channel, governor Roman Starovoit said that there were no casualties or damage.
