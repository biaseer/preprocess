Russian Grad rocket launchers destroy Ukrainian military sites

The footage shows how the GRAD systems change their stationing positions after firing and how then they move back to their positions to fire again at specific targets.
The Russian Defense Ministry said in a statement accompanying footage that the crews of the Grad rocket launchers carried out missions to destroy the positions of the Ukrainian Armed Forces.
Moscow, SANA- The Russian army, using Grad rocket launchers, destroyed sites of the Ukrainian armed forces.
