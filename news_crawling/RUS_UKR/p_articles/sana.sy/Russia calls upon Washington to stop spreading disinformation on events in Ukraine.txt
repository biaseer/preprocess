Russia calls upon Washington to stop spreading disinformation on events in Ukraine
20220317
http://sana.sy/en/?p=266639
Washington, SANA- Russia called upon the US to stop spreading disinformation and engaged in objective coverage of events in Ukraine.
The Embassy called upon the media to stop telling lies and start covering the events in Ukraine objectively, recommending people to use Russian sources for truthful information.
In response to US media allegations on Russia attack on Mariupol Theatre, the Russian Embassy in US accused American media of running a disinformation campaign on alleged involvement of Russian forces in bombing a theatre in Mariupol.
Meanwhile, Russian Ministry of Defense stressed that militants of the Azov nationalist battalion blew up the cultural theater.
