Russian MoD Ukrainian target destroyed with Onyx highprecision missile
20220508
http://sana.sy/en/?p=271387
Moscow, SANA- The Russian coastal defense missile system destroyed with Onyx high-precision missile a Ukrainian military target.
As part of the implementation of the tasks of the special military operation, the armed forces stroke with high-precision weapons the military infrastructure of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, the Ministry said in a statement.
Russian Defense Ministry released a footage on Sunday showing that the missile was launched from the Black Sea coast towards the Ukraine military target.
