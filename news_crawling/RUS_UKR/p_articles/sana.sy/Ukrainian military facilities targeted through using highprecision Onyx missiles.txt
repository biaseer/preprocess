The Bastion is a Russian coastal missile defense system developed in cooperation with Belarus and designed to counter various types of surface warships, including frigates, bombers, helicopter carriers, aircraft, landing ships, in addition to land targets, and it can operate in conditions of intense electronic jamming and obstruction.
Moscow, SANA-The Russian Ministry of Defense has released a footage which shows targeting Ukrainian military facilities with high-precision missiles.
The footage shows that a Bastion coastal missile system launched two Onyx missiles from the coast of the Black Sea towards land targets in the territory of Ukraine.
