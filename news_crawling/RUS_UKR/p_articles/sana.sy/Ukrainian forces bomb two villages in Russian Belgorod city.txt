Ukrainian forces bomb two villages in Russian Belgorod city
20220505
http://sana.sy/en/?p=271154
Since the beginning of the Russian special military operation to protect Donbass, several areas in the Belgorod region and other border regions with Ukraine have been bombarded by Ukrainian forces, causing human and material losses.
Moscow, SANA The authorities of Belgorod province, western Russia, announced, Thursday that the Ukrainian shelling targeted two villages in the province, causing damage and material losses.
Russian Today website quoted the provincial governor, Vyacheslav Gladkov, as saying that the villages of Zhuravlyovka and Nekhoteevka were bombed from the territory of Ukraine, confirming reports of the destruction of a house and a garage for cars as a result of the bombing.
