The committees officials will investigate the recent criminal acts of the Kyiv regime against citizens of Ukraine and in Donbass region, and the investigators will look into the crimes and prosecute those responsible for them, TASS news agency quoted the committees press service as saying.
Moscow, SANA- The Investigative Committee of the Russian Federation announced Monday its intention to investigate the blast that had injured four people in the southeastern Ukrainian city of Melitopol yesterday.
Four people were injured in Melitopol yesterday in an explosive device blast near the building of the Ministry of the Interior, and the Ukrainian forces bombed Donetsk region as a man was killed and five other civilians were wounded.
