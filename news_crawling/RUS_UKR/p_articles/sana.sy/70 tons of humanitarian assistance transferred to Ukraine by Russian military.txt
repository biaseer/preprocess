70 tons of humanitarian assistance transferred to Ukraine by Russian military
20220327
http://sana.sy/en/?p=267689
This assistance was transferred by three Il-76 military transport aircrafts from Russian Surgut city to Gomel city in the Republic of Belarus, after that it will be transported by trucks to Ukrainian Residential Centers, according to a statement by the ministry cited by RT Today website.
The ministry stated that this aid, which arrived in the form of convoys of trucks, will be distributed and transported soon to cities and villages in the Kyiv and Chernigov regions.
Moscow, SANA- Russian Defense Ministry announced Sunday transferring more than 70 tons of humanitarian assistance to be distributed to civilians in Ukraine
