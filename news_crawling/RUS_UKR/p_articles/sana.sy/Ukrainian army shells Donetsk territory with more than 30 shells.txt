Ukrainian army shells Donetsk territory with more than 30 shells
20220622
http://sana.sy/en/?p=275547
Russia Today website quoted the command as saying that the Ukrainian forces fired 24 120 mm mortar shells and seven 152 mm artillery shells on the territory of Donetsk.
Moscow, SANA- The command of the forces of the Donetsk Peoples Republic announced on Wednesday that the Ukrainian forces fired more than 30 shells on the territory of the republic during the past 24 hours.
The statement added that the shelling targeted the Kyivskyi and Petrovsky regions, as well as the towns of Golmovsky and Zaitsevo.
