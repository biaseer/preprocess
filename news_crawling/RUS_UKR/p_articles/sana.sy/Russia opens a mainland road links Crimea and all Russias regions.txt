Russia opens a mainland road links Crimea and all Russias regions
20220607

A mainland road link between Crimea and the rest of Russia is now available, and unimpeded water supplies to the Crimean Peninsula through the North Crimean channel have been resumed, Tass News Agency quoted Shoigu as saying
Moscow, SANA- Russian Defense Minister Sergei Shoigu, said at a conference call on Tuesday that a mainland road link is now available between Crimea and the rest of Russia.
Earlier, drivers could only reach to Crimea from mainland Russia through the Crimean Bridge that extends through the Strait of Kerch.
