Russian helicopters conduct missions to protect military convoys in Chernigov Ukraine
20220329
http://sana.sy/en/?p=267929
The footage shows the arrival of helicopters to the advanced positions of the Russian forces in Chernigov province, to participate in the protection of a convoy of heavy engineering equipment and trucks loaded with foodstuffs.
Helicopters, drones and reconnaissance units on armored cars Tiger and Typhoon carried out reconnaissance missions to ensure the safety of the convoy, as shown by the footage.
Moscow, SANA- The Russian Ministry of Defense released a footage which shows the crews of the Russian helicopters carrying out combat missions in the vicinity of the Ukrainian province of Chernigov as part of the special operation to protect Donbass.
