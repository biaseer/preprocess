Russian Defense releases footage showing destruction of Ukrainian armored vehicles military depots
20220320
http://sana.sy/en/?p=266895
Moscow, SANA- Russian Ministry of Defense released footage on Sunday showing the destruction of armored vehicles and military depots of Ukrainian Army by using Russian-made drones.
Sputnik quoted the Ministrys military administration as saying that the missiles strikes targeting the military equipment and ammunition depots were carried out from medium and low heights, adding that the destructions of the targets was carried out by drones.
A video footage showed several attacks that were carried out by using Different types of attack drones, including Forpost attack drone.
