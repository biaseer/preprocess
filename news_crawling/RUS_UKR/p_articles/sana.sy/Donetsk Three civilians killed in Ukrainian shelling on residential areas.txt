The forces command announced on Thursday that 4 civilians were killed and 7 others were wounded as a result of continuing the Ukrainian shelling on its land.
Donetsk, SANA-The leadership of Donetsk Peoples Republic said that three people were killed in Ukrainian forces bombing of residential centers on its territories.
The Ukrainian shelling also caused the injury of 17 civilian , the Russian Ria Novosti quoted the forces command as saying in a statement Saturday.
