The footage shows the progress of a convoy of Russian armored vehicles on the Y95 road after leaving Zalissia town, about 40 kilometers from Kyiv, and another convoy standing on the side of the road, which includes at least 10 Russian armored vehicles.
The convoy runs within a strong circle of combat protection, under a cover by helicopters, drones and reconnaissance groups on Tiger armored cars.
Moscow, SANA- The Russian Ministry of Defense released a footage on Monday that showed the progress of the Russian airdrop units on armored vehicles in Ukrainian province of Kyiv Oblast , crossing water obstacles and destroying two enemy marches.
The Russian Ministry of Defense also released footage showing the Russian material and technical supply units conducting a combat march towards the advanced positions of the Russian forces in Chernigov Province.
The ministry said that during their advance, personnel of the Russian units crossed a water barrier on a temporary bridge and continued their advance.
The ministry stated that a mobile Russian radio radar station attached to the Strela air defense missile system discovered a reconnaissance drone and a combat drone and they were destroyed.
The footage shows a convoy that includes fuel tanks, heavy engineering equipment, tanks, and trucks loaded with foodstuffs to complete the reserves.
