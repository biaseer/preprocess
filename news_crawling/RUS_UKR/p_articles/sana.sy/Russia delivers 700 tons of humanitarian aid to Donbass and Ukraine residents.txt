Russia delivers 700 tons of humanitarian aid to Donbass and Ukraine residents

RIA Novosti Agency quoted the Russian Emergencies as saying in a statement on Monday that Russia has delivered over 700 tons of humanitarian aid to Donbass and Ukraine through the rescue centers of the ministry.
Moscow, SANA- Russia has delivered more than 700 tons of humanitarian aid to the residents of Donbass and several regions of Ukraine.
