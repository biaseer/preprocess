Russian soldiers start clearing mines from Ukraines Azovstal steel mill
20220523
https://www.business-standard.com/article/international/russian-soldiers-start-clearing-mines-from-ukraine-s-azovstal-steel-mill-122052300151_1.html
Russian soldiers cleared mines and debris on the industrial grounds of the Azovstal steelworks in Mariupol on Sunday after hundreds of Ukrainian forces holed up in the vast plant for weeks were ordered to stand down.
