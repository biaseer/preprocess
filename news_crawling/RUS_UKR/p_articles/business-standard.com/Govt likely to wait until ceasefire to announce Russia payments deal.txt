Govt likely to wait until ceasefire to announce Russia payments deal
20220411
https://www.business-standard.com/article/economy-policy/govt-likely-to-wait-until-ceasefire-to-announce-russia-payments-deal-122041000961_1.html
The Centre is unlikely to announce an agreement over payment mechanism with Russia until a ceasefire is declared in the Ukraine war, said people in the know. An inter-ministerial group headed by the finance ministry has been meeting regularly for over a month to analyse and discuss payment mechanisms that can work under the current circumstances and ensure the trade between India and Russia is not disrupted.
