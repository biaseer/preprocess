Canada to refer ICC to examine alleged Russian war crimes in Ukraine
20220302
https://www.business-standard.com/article/international/canada-to-refer-icc-to-examine-alleged-russian-war-crimes-in-ukraine-122030200223_1.html
Canada has decided to refer the situation in Ukraine to the International Criminal Court ICC to probe alleged war crimes and crimes against humanity by Russian forces in Ukraine, its government said on Tuesday.
