Russian missiles rain on Ukrainian towns as war nears halfyear mark

https://www.business-standard.com/article/international/russian-missiles-rain-on-ukrainian-towns-as-war-nears-half-year-mark-122082201357_1.html
Russia fired rockets at towns to the west of Europes largest nuclear power plant in southern Ukraine on Monday while the capital Kyiv banned rallies this week to commemorate independence from Soviet rule for fear of Russian attacks, as Russias war on Ukraine neared the half-year mark.
