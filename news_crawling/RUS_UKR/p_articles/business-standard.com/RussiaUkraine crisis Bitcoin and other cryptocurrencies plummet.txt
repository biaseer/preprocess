Bitcoin slumped to its lowest in a month on Thursday after Russian forces fired missiles at several cities in Ukraine and landed troops on its coast, sparking a sell-off of riskier assets. Cryptocurrencies extended declines as Russias attacks on targets across Ukraine sent risk assets reeling, with Bitcoin slumping to a one-month low.
