Erdogan to visit Kiev on Thursday to mediate between Russia and Ukraine

"The visit of our President to Ukraine is expected on 3 February," Erdogan's administration said.
Last week, Erdogan also stated that "a psychology of war in the region upsets us, as a country that has ties with both sides. Our wish is to bring Mr. Putin and Mr. Zelenskiy together as soon as possible".
According to the report, the Turkish President will visit Ukraine to ease tensions in the region.
Turkish President, Recep Tayyip Erdogan, plans to visit Ukraine on 3 February, TASS reports, citing the office of the Turkish leader.
While Turkey has made no direct military allegiance with either Kiev or Moscow and has maintained good relations with both, it hassuppliedUkraine with armed combat drones and haswarnedRussia against an invasion.
Earlier, the Turkish leader hadrepeatedly stated that Turkey was ready for mediation efforts and a feasible contribution to peace between Ukraine and Russia.
