KYIV. Feb 26 Interfax - Ukraine and Russia are holding consultations on the venue and time of talks, Ukrainian presidential spokesman Serhiy Nykyforov said.
"Hello colleagues! I should refute statements that we have refused to hold talks. Ukraine has been ready and remains ready to discuss a ceasefire and peace. This is our permanent position. We responded by agreeing to the proposal of the president of the Russian Federation. The parties are in consultations on the venue and time of the negotiations during these hours," he said on Facebook on Friday evening.
Russian Foreign Ministry spokesperson Maria Zakharova said that Kyiv had refused to negotiate and suggested that the issue be postponed until Saturday.
