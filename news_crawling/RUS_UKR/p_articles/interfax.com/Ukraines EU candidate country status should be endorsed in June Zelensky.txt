Ukraines EU candidate country status should be endorsed in June  Zelensky
20220531
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/79694/
MOSCOW. May 31 Interfax - Ukraine's status as a candidate country for membership in the European Union should be endorsed in June, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky in a speech to the European Council members by video link on Monday evening.
"European Union candidate country status for Ukraine should be endorsed at the strategic level as soon as June," Ukrainian media outlets quoted Zelensky as saying.
"We received the candidate status questionnaire on April 8 and we have completed it, we have given answers to the European Commission. We count on the same reaction from Europe and we cast aside any alternative that no one needs. It is necessary for us to be like you. We have fulfilled all criteria for this status," he said.
