Trade blockade of Donbas is now over  Lavrov
20220225
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/74304/
MOSCOW. Feb 25 Interfax - Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov has said he is hoping that Kyiv's trade blockade of Donbas is now over.
"Hopefully, all restrictions associated with the trade blockade declared by Kyiv are gone. We have discussed practical tasks deriving from the treaties of friendship and cooperation, first and foremost, the drafting of agreements on every area of our interaction," Lavrov said at a press conference in Moscow.
