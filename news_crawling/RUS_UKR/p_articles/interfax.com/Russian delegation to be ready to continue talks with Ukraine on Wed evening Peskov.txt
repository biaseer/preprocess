MOSCOW. March 2 Interfax - The Russian delegation will be waiting for the Ukrainian delegation to continue talks on Wednesday afternoon, Russian presidential press secretary Dmitry Peskov said.
"Our delegation will be waiting for the Ukrainian negotiators on-site this afternoon closer to the evening," Peskov said.
Russian presidential aide Vladimir Medinsky, who led the Russian delegation in the first round of the talks, said the next meeting will be held on the Polish-Belarusian border.
