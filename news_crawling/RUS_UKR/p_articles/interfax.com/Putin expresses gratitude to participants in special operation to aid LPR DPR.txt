Putin expresses gratitude to participants in special operation to aid LPR DPR
20220227
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/74561/
"I pay special tribute to those heroically performing their military duty during the special operation to provide assistance to the people's republic of Donbas these days," Putin said in his greetings message to members and veterans of the special operations forces, congratulating them on their professional day.
MOSCOW. Feb 27 Interfax - Russian President Vladimir Putin has expressed gratitude to participants in the special operation providing assistance to the Donetsk People's Republic DPR and Luhansk People's Republic LPR.
