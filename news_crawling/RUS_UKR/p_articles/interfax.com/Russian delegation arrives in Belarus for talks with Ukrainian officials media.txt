MOSCOW. March 7 Interfax - The Russian delegation has arrived at the place of the talks with Ukraine, the Belarusian agency BelTA has reported.
Mykhailo Podolyak, an adviser to the head of the Office of the President of Ukraine, earlier said members of the delegations in the talks on the situation between Russia and Ukraine would not state the proposed conditions until "common positions" were agreed upon.
