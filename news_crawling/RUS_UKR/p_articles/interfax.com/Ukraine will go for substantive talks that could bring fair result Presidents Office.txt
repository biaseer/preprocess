KYIV. Feb 27 Interfax - Talking about talks, Russia is trying to make ultimatum requests to Ukraine, advisor to the head of the Ukrainian President's Office Mykhailo Podoliak said.
Ukraine can accept "an agreed format of talks, an agreed venue, real substance and a fair result," but only if Russia stops manipulations around these talks, he said.
