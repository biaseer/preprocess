Russian Defense Ministry releases video of Su34 attack plane striking on Ukrainian military target with highprecision weapons
20220306
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/75758/
MOSCOW. March 6 Interfax - The Russian Defense Ministry on Sunday released a video showing a mission of the Russian Aerospace Forces' operational and tactical aircraft during the special military operation in Ukraine.
"The crew of a Sukhoi Su-34 plane armed with high-precision airborne weapons destroyed a military infrastructure facility of the paramilitary forces of Ukrainian nationalists," the text attached to the footage says.
