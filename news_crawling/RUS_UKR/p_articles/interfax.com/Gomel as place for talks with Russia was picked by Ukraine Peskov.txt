MOSCOW. Feb 27 Interfax - The Ukrainian side itself has proposed Gomel as the place for talks with Russia, Russian presidential press secretary Dmitry Peskov told reporters.
"The city of Gomel was proposed by the Ukrainian side - the Ukrainian side itself - as the place for talks. As we said earlier, the Russian delegation is ready for these talks in the place. Russian presidential aide Vladimir Medinsky is leading the Russian delegation. He will make a relevant statement soon," Peskov said.
