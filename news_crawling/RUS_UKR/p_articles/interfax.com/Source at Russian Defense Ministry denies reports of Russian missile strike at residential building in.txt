Source at Russian Defense Ministry denies reports of Russian missile strike at residential building in Kyiv
20220226
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/74453/
MOSCOW. Feb 26 Interfax - The Russian military sources have denied reports of a missile strike at a residential building in Kyiv.
"Information about a Russian missile strike at a residential building on Lobanovskyi Avenue in Kyiv circulating on social networks is not true. The nature of the damage done to the building shows that a surface-to-air missile had hit it," a source at the Russian Defense Ministry said on Saturday.
