Russian Foreign Ministry advises compatriots in Ukraine to seek consular assistance from agencies in neighboring states
20220225
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/74324/
MOSCOW. Feb 25 Interfax - Russian citizens in Ukraine can seek consular assistance from the neighboring countries or agencies on the territory of Russia, Russian Foreign Ministry spokesperson Maria Zakharova said.
"As to Russian citizens who remain in Ukraine ... I want to say that, after the evacuation of Russian diplomatic missions, which were directly threatened by nationalists, they can ... if they need turn to relevant agencies, establishments on the territory of the Russian Federation, or turn to the consular establishments of our country in the neighboring states to receive consular services," Zakharova told a briefing on Friday.
