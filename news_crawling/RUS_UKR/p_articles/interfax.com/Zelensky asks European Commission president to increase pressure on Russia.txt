Zelensky asks European Commission president to increase pressure on Russia
20220225
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/74240/
KYIV. Feb 25 Interfax - Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky has thanked European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen for the additional financial assistance to Ukraine and declared the need for stronger sanctions against Russia.
"Not all possibilities for sanctions have been exhausted yet. The pressure on Russia must increase. Said this to Ursula von der Leyen. I am grateful to the President for her decision on additional financial assistance," Zelensky said on Twitter on Friday.
