Moscow considers even thought of war between Ukraine and Russia unacceptable
20220127
https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/73730/
MOSCOW. Jan 27 Interfax - There is relative calm on the contact line in Donbas, Alexei Zaitsev, deputy director of the Russian Foreign Ministry's Information and Press Department, said.
"We have said more than once that our country is not going to attack anyone," Zaitsev said.
"We believe that even the thought of a war between our peoples is unacceptable," he said.
"We state relative calm on the contact line in Donbas, the number of ceasefire breaches has declined from the first weeks of January," he told a briefing in Moscow on Thursday, citing reports from the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe OSCE Special Monitoring Mission.
