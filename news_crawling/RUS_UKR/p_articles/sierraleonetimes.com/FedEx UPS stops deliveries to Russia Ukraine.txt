FedEx UPS stops deliveries to Russia Ukraine
20220302
https://www.sierraleonetimes.com/news/272352135/fedex-ups-stops-deliveries-to-russia-ukraine
NEW YORK CITY, New York: FedEx and the United Parcel Service are the latest companies to announce they are halting deliveries, less than a week after Russia invaded Ukraine.On its website, FedEX said it is not providing services in and out of Ukraine until further notice, and shipments already in transit will be stored temporarily.The United Parcel Service UPS is also adjusting its services, with all shipping to, from and within Ukraine halted from 24th February until further notice, as well as international shipments to destination addresses in Russia from 25th February until further notice.
