MENAFN- UkrinForm The Ukrainian Interior Ministry plans to initiate an increase in the number of bomb disposal experts before the government. That's according to Ukraine's First Deputy Interior Minister Yevhenii Yenin, Ukrinform reports.

'Ukrainian State Emergency Service units are actively demining the territory. Every day our rescuers dispose of about 5,000 unexploded ordnance and aerial bombs. We are stepping up this activity and plan to initiate an increase in the number of bomb disposal experts before the Government of Ukraine. This will ensure the maximum acceleration of work to dispose of all ammunition left after Russian aggression,' Yenin said.
