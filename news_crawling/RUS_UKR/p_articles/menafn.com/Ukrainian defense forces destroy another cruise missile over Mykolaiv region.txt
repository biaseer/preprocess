On February 24, Russian President Vladimir Putin announced the beginning of a full-scale invasion of Ukraine. Russian troops have been shelling and destroying key infrastructure facilities, conducting massive shelling of residential areas of Ukrainian cities and villages using artillery, multiple rocket launchers and ballistic missiles.
MENAFN- UkrinForm In Mykolaiv region, an air defense unit of the 80th separate airborne assault brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine shot down another Russian cruise missile launched on peaceful Ukrainians. The Command of the Air Assault Troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine wrote this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports.
