Ukraine  Occupiers fire mortars near Vodiane and Pisky

MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, February 6, the Russian-occupation troops violated the ceasefire in the Joint Forces Operation JFO area in eastern Ukraine twice. Towards Vodiane, Donetsk region, the occupiers opened fire from hand-held antitank grenade launchers. Outside Pisky 11km north-west of Donetsk, the Russian mercenaries fired grenade machine guns on Ukrainian defenders, the JFO Headquarters press center posted on Facebook.
