The announcement also mentioned that Russia has also lost 2,218 vehicles, 13 ships as well as light boats, 491 unmanned aerial vehicles together with 112 cruise missiles.
MENAFN On Wednesday the Ukrainian Defense Ministry stated that more than 29,450 Russian militaries so far have been killed throughout the conflict in Ukraine.
In line with a declaration by the Ukrainian General Staff about 100 Russian militaries were murdered during the past 24 hours.
It stated that Ukrainian forces have ruined 206 planes, 170 helicopters, 1,305 battle tanks, 3,213 reinforced vehicles, 606 artilleries, 201 rocket launchers, as well as 93 air-defense systems since the war broke out.
