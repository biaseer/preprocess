MENAFN- UkrinForm Head of the Ukrainian President's Office Andriy Yermak had a phone call with U.S. National Security Adviser Jake Sullivan as part of their permanent dialogue, the President's Office has said. According to the report , both parties discussed the urgent issues and needs of Ukraine in order to strengthen the country's defense capability and resilience in repelling Russia's all-out invasion.
