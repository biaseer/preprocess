MENAFN- UkrinForm As of March 4, 2022, due to enemy shelling, a total of 13 gas distribution stations were disconnected, and hundreds of consumers were left without gas supplies. The relevant statement was made by Gas Transmission System Operator of Ukraine LLC GTSOU on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.

In particular, this refers to Irpin, Borodianka, Hostomel, Stoianka, Voznesensk, partially Chernihiv, Izium, Brazhkivka, Syrotyno, Krasnopopivka, Donske, Novotroitske, Prystan, Oleshky and many other settlements located within the line of fire.
In general, Russian troops continue to attack Ukraine's critical infrastructure, causing a humanitarian catastrophe across Ukrainian cities.
As of March 4, 2022, due to shelling by the Russian military, a total of 13 gas distribution stations are remaining disconnected. Dozens of Ukrainian cities and villages with hundreds of consumers have been left without gas supplies, the report states.
The relevant statement was made by Gas Transmission System Operator of Ukraine LLC GTSOU on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
