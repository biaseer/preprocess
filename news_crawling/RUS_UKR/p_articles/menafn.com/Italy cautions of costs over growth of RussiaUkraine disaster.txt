MENAFN After discussions with the US and its European allies, Italy rang a bell that Russia would risk serious consequences from a rise of the frontier disaster with Ukraine.
Italian Premier also said that the discussion addressed support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine was reiterated, and members pointed up the importance of maintaining the closest coordination between the allies and the need for a common response.
Draghi emphasized this discussion should at the same time clarify the serious consequences that could derive from a further deterioration of the situation,, while stating that the video conference was planned by US Leader Joe Biden.
Italian Premier Mario Draghi stated that he held a video call with his US, French, German, British and Polish premiers and European Union and NATO presidents, and all of them confirmed the importance to keep open a channel of dialogue with Russia to ease tensions.
