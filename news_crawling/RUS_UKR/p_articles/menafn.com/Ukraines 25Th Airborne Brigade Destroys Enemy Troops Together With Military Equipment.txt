MENAFN- UkrinForm The 25th Separate Sicheslavska Airborne Brigade of the Ukrainian Air Assault Forces has destroyed a group of Russian occupiers and their military equipment. The relevant statement was made by the Air Assault Forces Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
The 25th Separate Sicheslavska Airborne Brigade of the Ukrainian Air Assault Forces is destroying Russian occupiers together with military equipment. The enemy infantry fighting vehicle and 'lost' occupiers followed the route of the Russian warship, the report states.
A reminder that today Ukrainian paratroopers have also shot down Russia's ZALA 421-08 micro UAV designed to conduct reconnaissance and adjust the fire.
