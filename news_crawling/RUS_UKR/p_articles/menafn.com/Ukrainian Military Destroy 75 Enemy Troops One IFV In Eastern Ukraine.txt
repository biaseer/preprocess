MENAFN- UkrinForm On April 27, 2022, within the control area of the East Operational Tactical Group, Russian troops launched three attacks and suffered losses. The relevant statement was made by the East Operational Tactical Group on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
Battles are raging at the moment. Over the past day, Ukrainian defenders have destroyed 75 Russian troops, one infantry fighting vehicle IFV, two motor vehicles and one unmanned aerial vehicle UAV.
The relevant statement was made by the East Operational Tactical Group on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
