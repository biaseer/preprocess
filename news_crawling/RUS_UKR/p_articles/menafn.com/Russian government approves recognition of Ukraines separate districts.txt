At an another unexpected meeting, politicians solidly stood with the choice of Russian Leader Vladimir Putin, that approved Russia's agreements of relation, collaboration, and mutual help with the dissenters.
Valentina Matvienko, the ruler of the Council of Federation, noted on the decision by stating it is "Russia's way to stop the civil war in Ukraine."
MENAFN Both meeting rooms of the Russian government, the Council of Federation and the State Duma, totally permitted the recognition of Ukraine's separate areas of Donetsk and Luhansk as independent countries.
According to a report, Kremlin spokesperson Dmitry Peskov declared Russia recognized Donetsk and Luhansk "within the borders they proclaimed themselves."
