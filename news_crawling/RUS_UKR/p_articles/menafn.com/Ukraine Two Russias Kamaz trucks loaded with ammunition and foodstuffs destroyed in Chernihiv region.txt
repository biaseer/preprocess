MENAFN- UkrinForm Ukrainian resistance forces destroyed two Russian trucks carrying ammunition and foodstuffs in Chernihiv region. 'Ammunition and foodstuffs will not reach the occupier. Minus two Kamaz trucks. The resistance forces of Chernihiv region are working,' the Operational Command 'North' posted on Facebook.
Earlier, Ukrainian resistance forces destroyed a Russian column and took the Uragan multiple rocket launcher system as a trophy near Pryluky.
