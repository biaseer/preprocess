More Than 170 Invaders Eliminated 4 Tanks Destroyed In Eastern Ukraine

On April 29, Russian troops have launched four attacks in eastern Ukraine. Ukrainian defenders have eliminated 173 Russian invaders and destroyed four tanks, six infantry fighting vehicles, two armored personnel vehicles, 13 vehicles, five artillery systems, two mortar squads, and five unmanned aerial vehicles, the operational and tactical groupEast posted on Facebook .
As reported, the Armed Forces of Ukraine have eliminated about 23,000 Russian invaders since the beginning of the full-scale war.
