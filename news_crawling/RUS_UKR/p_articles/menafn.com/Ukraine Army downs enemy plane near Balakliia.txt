MENAFN- UkrinForm One more enemy aircraft has been shot down by the Armed Forces of Ukraine in Kharkiv Region. The relevant statement was made by Kharkiv Regional Military Administration Head Oleh Syniehubov during a telethon, an Ukrinform correspondent reports.

In his words, Russian occupiers continue to shell the city of Kharkiv. Today Russian shells have hit Kharkiv's Nemyshlianskyi District and Osnovianskyi District, leaving three civilians killed. Such districts as Piatykhatky, Pivnichna Saltivka and downtown are also affected by Russia's shelling.
According to the estimates of the law enforcement agencies, more than 500 civilians have been killed in Kharkiv Region over 50 days since the Russian invasion started.
Today we have shot down an enemy plane near the city of Balakliia, Syniehubov told.
