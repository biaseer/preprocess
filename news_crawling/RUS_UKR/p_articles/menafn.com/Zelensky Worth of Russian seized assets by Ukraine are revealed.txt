On August 5, Kiev approved a decree authorizing the transfer of 903 Russian-owned assets in the country to the state and Ukrainian people. According to Prime Minister Denis Shmygal, the property that Ukraine intends to seize is divided into three categories: assets belonging to the Russian state, sanctioned Russian citizens and companies, and Russian banks.
According to Zelensky, the confiscatedassets will be used to compensate for the damages caused by the country's current military conflict.
MENAFN Ukraine has seized $765 million in Russian assets in the country, Ukrainian President Vladimir Zelensky announced on Saturday on his Telegram channel.
Held a meeting today on sanctions policy, confiscation of Russian assets. As government officials reported, assets worth 28 billion hryvnias about $765 million have already been forcibly seized. This work continues. More than 900 more facilities belonging to the Russian state are proposed to be confiscated. And if we evaluate the property package not only of the Russian state, but also of its residents, we are talking about 36 thousand items to be seized, he asserted.
