According to available information, Zhytomyr region was attacked from the south-eastern direction by sea-launched cruise missiles.
Today, May 22, at about 13:00, the enemy launched a missile strike on the infrastructure of Zhytomyr region. The air defense units of the Air Command 'Center' downed four cruise missiles, the Air CommandCenter posted on Facebook , Ukrinform reports.
It is noted that three missiles were destroyed by aircraft, and another one  by a unit of anti-aircraft missile forces.
As reported, the Air Defense Forces of Ukraine shot down a Russian missile over Fastiv district of Kyiv region on Sunday. According to preliminary information, there are no casualties or damage.
