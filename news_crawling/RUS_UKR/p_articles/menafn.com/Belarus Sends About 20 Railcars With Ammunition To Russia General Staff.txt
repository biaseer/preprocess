MENAFN- UkrinForm Belarus has sent up to 20 railcars with ammunition to Russia for the war against Ukraine. According to Ukrinform, the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said this on Facebook .
In the Donetsk direction, Russian troops with the support of artillery are trying to block the city of Lysychansk from the south.
