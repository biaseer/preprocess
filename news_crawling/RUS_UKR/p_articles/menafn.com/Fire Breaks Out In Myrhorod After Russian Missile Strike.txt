MENAFN- UkrinForm A fire broke out at a military facility in Myrhorod, Poltava region, following a Russian missile strike. 'Today, at 11:00, the troops of the Russian Federation launched a group strike with air-to-surface missiles, probably Kh-22, on the military infrastructure of the town of Myrhorod. The fire rages,' the press service of the Myrhorod Town Council posted on Telegram .
