The globes largest oil suppliers in the OPEC group are finding it extremely difficult to hit their monthly output ratios. Thus, oil markets are currently undergoing constraints when it comes to supply which disables them from meeting demands.
MENAFN On Monday, oil costs surged amidst the escalating conflicts in Eastern Europe, with Russian soldiers on the verge of striking Ukraine, thus, accelerating concerns of penalties against Russia, which is the bloc's most significant oil supplier.
Meanwhile, the American benchmark West Texas Intermediate WTI sold each barrel for USD94.34, increasing by 1.33 percent from the last session's close of USD93.10 per barrel.
