MENAFN- UkrinForm The Ukrainian anti-aircraft defense units have shot down a Russian cruise missile over Odesa Region. The relevant statement was made by the South Operational Command on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
Another missile strike was launched on Odesa Region. The enemy aircraft fired a cruise missile, which was accurately shot down by the anti-aircraft defense units over the sea, the report states.
The relevant statement was made by the South Operational Command on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
A reminder that, on May 10, 2022, Russians launched the fourth missile strike on the bridge over the Dniester Estuary in Odesa Region.
