Ukraine  Occupiers violate ceasefire in JFO area four times
20211219
https://menafn.com/1103394179/Ukraine-Occupiers-violate-ceasefire-in-JFO-area-four-times&source=138
MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, December 17, Russian-occupation troops violated the ceasefire in the Joint Forces Operation JFO area in eastern Ukraine four times. Towards Travneve 51km north-east of Donetsk, the occupiers fired grenade machine guns. Outside Novotoshkivske 53km west of Luhansk, the enemy opened fire from grenade launchers of different systems and small arms. Towards Novoluhanske 53km north-east of Donetsk, the Russian mercenaries launched two attacks, using grenade machine guns and small arms, the JFO Headquarters press center posted on Facebook.
