A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and June 7, 2022, Russia's total combat losses in Ukraine reached about 31,360 troops.
Russian invaders opened fire on 16 settlements in Donetsk Region and Luhansk Region, having damaged or destroyed 39 civilian objects, including 36 residential houses, school, nursery school and power transmission line. One civilian was killed and six injured.
In particular, the Ukrainian military destroyed three tanks, five artillery systems, two armored fighting vehicles, 13 motor vehicles and three ammunition depots. Battles are still raging within two more locations.
