Three Civilians Killed In Donetsk Region Over Past Day
20220815

MENAFN- UkrinForm Russian troops killed three civilians and injured 13 more in Donetsk region over the past day, August 14. 'On August 14, the Russians killed three civilians of Donetsk region: in Bakhmut, Soledar, and Marinka. Thirteen more people were injured,' Pavlo Kyrylenko, Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration, posted on Telegram .
