After taking control of the power station on March 4, Russian troops have arrested 26 violators of access control to the plant  the biggest nuclear power plant in Europe, the National Guard publicized. The facility carries on working with local staff, while Russian troops patrol the buildings and check for explosive devices and unexploded munitions, the National Guard said.

MENAFN Russian forces have arrested two staffs of the Zaporozhye Nuclear Power Plant in southern Ukraine, who have been giving info on troops and hardware locations to Kievs militaries, the Russian National Guard Rosgvardiya declared on Wednesday.
The officials continued that Specialists of the nuclear, biological and chemical defense of the National Guard, together with colleagues from the Ministry of Defense, daily, several times a day, monitor the situation by measuring radiation levels at control points along the perimeter of the facility.
Apart from that, a violator of access control to the plant, an accomplice of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, who transmitted the coordinates of the movement of columns of Russian military hardware, has been detained, they also said.
