Kharkiv Regions Gas Processing Enterprise Hit With Russian Missiles
20220618

In Izium District, Russian occupiers fired at a gas processing enterprise. According to the preliminary data, several Russian missiles hit the target. Detached houses were also damaged. A massive fire broke out, Syniehubov wrote.

MENAFN- UkrinForm In Izium District, Kharkiv Region, the Russian military fired missiles at a gas processing enterprise, having caused a massive fire. The relevant statement was made by Kharkiv Regional Military Administration Head Oleh Syniehubov on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
