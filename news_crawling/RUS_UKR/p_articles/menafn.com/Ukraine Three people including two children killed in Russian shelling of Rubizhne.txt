MENAFN- UkrinForm On March 19, three people, including two children, were killed last night in Rubizhne, Luhansk region, as a result of enemy shelling. This was announced on Telegram by the head of the Luhansk Regional Military Administration, Serhiy Haidai , Ukrinform reports.
Currently, 23 settlements remain completely cut off gas supply and another seven  partially, which left 35,000 people without gas. In 26 settlements, electricity supplies have been severed and in 12 they remain limited. A total of 98,364 people have been deprived of electricity, with 627 transformer power substations having been shut down.
