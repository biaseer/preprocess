Russia and Ukraine, which are both main worldwide grain exporters, had a share of about 30 percent of world wheat exports in 2021.
MENAFN Trkiye is going to negotiate with Russia and Ukraine decisions to guarantee exports of Ukrainian grain to the world, on the words of the Turkish leader.
At a conference with youths in the eastern province of Van, Recep Tayyip Erdogan announced he would talk over the problem of a grain export corridor with Russian Leader Vladimir Putin and Ukrainian Leader Volodymyr Zelenskyy.
Western and Russian officials are cautioning of a worldwide food insecurity because of the conflict in Ukraine and restrictions against Russia.
