"Gazprom will supply 46.8 million cubic meters of natural gas via Ukraine on May 15 Monday," Kupriyanov went further on, relative to 62.7 million cubic meters on Sunday.
MENAFN Gazprom's spokesman Sergey Kupriyanov confirmed in a statement that the capacity of natural gas from Russian gas giant Gazprom intended for Europe through Ukraine is going to decline 25 percent on Monday in comparison with the amount the prior day.
Some 30 percent of gas exports for Europe transfer through Sokhranivka, in the Donbas district of eastern Ukraine.
