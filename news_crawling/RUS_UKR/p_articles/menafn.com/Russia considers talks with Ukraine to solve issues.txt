The representative stated to journalists at a media briefing in Russia, We welcome the efforts of any countries that can help resolve the situation in Ukraine. The main disagreements between Russia and Ukraine are connected precisely with the stalling of the implementation of the Minsk agreements by Kyiv.
While explaining the suggestion by Turkeys president Recep Tayyip Erdogan to prepare a conference combining Russias and Ukraines presidents, Peskov reported Russia does not oppose any initiation to resolve the case in Ukraine.
MENAFN On Wednesday, Kremlin representative Dmitry Peskov said, Russia will only entice works if Turkish associates can inspire Kyiv to apply the Minsk Protocol.
He added, If our Turkish partners can influence Ukrainians and encourage them to fulfill their earlier agreements and commitments, this will be welcome.
