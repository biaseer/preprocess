MENAFN- UkrinForm The Control Center of the Russian Space Agency Roskosmos has no more control over its spy-sattelites. Hacking group NB65, affiliated with Anonymous, has shut down the Control Center of the Russian Space Agency 'Roskosmos'. Russia has no more control over their own spy-sattelites, reads a message  on Telegram channel of the latest information from Ukraine's Armed Forces.
