MENAFN As the violence engulfs merchant transportation, Russia's invasion of Ukraine has wreaked havoc on commodity markets.
Since Russian military launched an attack on its neighbor this week, at least three commerce ships have reportedly been targeted. Guarantors are either refusing to cover vessels heading into the Black Sea or charging exorbitant prices.
Products from Russia should continue to be shipped in the long run, according to trade specialists, but prudence is likely in the short term, and the scenario is rapidly shifting.
