On Saturday morning, residents in the Ukrainian capital awoke to air raid sirens, following a day in which Moscow appeared to consolidate its forces on the outskirts of Kyiv, where significant fighting had taken place.
Ukrainian towns encircled by Russian soldiers came under fire once more. A mosque housing 80 civilians in Mariupol, where a humanitarian calamity is developing, has been shelled, as per the Ukrainian administration.
According to British intelligence, on Saturday Russian ground soldiers were 25 kilometers outside of the capital.
