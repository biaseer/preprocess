MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, December 13, Russian-occupation troops violated the ceasefire in eastern Ukraine twice. Near Popasna 69km west of Luhansk, the occupiers fired grenade launchers of different systems and heavy machine guns. Outside Katerynivka 64km west of Luhansk, the enemy opened fire from grenade launchers of different systems and small arms, the Defense Ministry of Ukraine informs.
