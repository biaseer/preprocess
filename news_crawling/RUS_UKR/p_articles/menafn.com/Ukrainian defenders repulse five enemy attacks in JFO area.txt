In particular, Ukrainian defenders destroyed 15 artillery systems, ten tanks, 18 armored vehicles and 13 vehicles.
Air defense units also shot down one enemy aircraft, three drones and one Russian cruise missile in eastern Ukraine.
MENAFN- UkrinForm Ukrainian defenders successfully repulsed five attacks by Russian invaders in the area of the Joint Forces Operation on March 30. According to Ukrinform, the press service of the Joint Forces Operation announced this on Facebook.
'Thanks to the skillful actions of the Joint Forces grouping, five enemy attacks have been successfully repulsed so far. Our soldiers have inflicted losses on the Russian occupiers,' the statement said.
