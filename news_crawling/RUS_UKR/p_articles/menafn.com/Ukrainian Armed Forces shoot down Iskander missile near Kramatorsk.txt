MENAFN- UkrinForm Russian troops this morning fired a missile from the Iskander system towards Kramatorsk, the Ukrainian Interior Ministry has said on Telegram. The missile was shot down, and its tail fell on the territory of the Shpychkyne station in the village of Yasnohirka, the report said.
On March 9, in the Donetsk region, Russian invaders fired mortars at the village of Novoluhanske, damaging houses, a gas pipeline and power lines.
According to preliminary information, no one was injured. Bomb disposal experts are working at the scene.
