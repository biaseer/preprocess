Shortly after Putin's public order not to storm the Mariupol-based Azovstal plant, Russian Defense Minister Sergei Shoigu announced that Mariupol was returning to 'peaceful life', and they were making continuous attempts to reach an agreement on evacuation with the Ukrainian military.
In fact, storming operations have not stopped for a day. Rashists were alternating artillery strikes with attempts to enter the territory of the Azovstal plant, the CCD stressed.
MENAFN- UkrinForm The Center for Countering Disinformation CCD at the Ukrainian National Security and Defense Council NSDC has warned of Russia's another manipulation. The relevant statement was made by the CCD on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
A reminder that, in the evening of May 4, 2022, Ukrainian authorities recovered connection with Mariupol defenders, remaining within the Azovstal plant. Reportedly, Russian troops had already entered the plant's territory.
