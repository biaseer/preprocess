The UK administration on Friday greeted the breakthrough grain agreement inked by Russia, Ukraine, the UN as well as Turkey to continue grain shipments via the Black Sea.
The US named Ukraine grain agreement signed in Istanbul a 'positive step,' and showed gratitude to leader Erdogan, UN chief Antonio Guterres for their efforts.
Foreign Secretary Liz Truss stated in an announcement that "Russian President Vladimir Putin's barbaric invasion of Ukraine has meant some of the poorest and most vulnerable people in the world are at risk of having nothing to eat."
