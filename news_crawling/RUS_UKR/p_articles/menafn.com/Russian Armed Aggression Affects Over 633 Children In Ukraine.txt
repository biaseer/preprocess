Russian Armed Aggression Affects Over 633 Children In Ukraine
20220508
https://menafn.com/1104173987/Russian-Armed-Aggression-Affects-Over-633-Children-In-Ukraine&source=138
Over 633 children in Ukraine have been affected by the Russian armed aggression. As of May 7, 2022, the number of those killed remained unchanged 223 children. The number of those injured increased to 410, the report states.
On May 4, 2022, a column of private cars, driving from Vovchansk Chuhuiv District, Kharkiv Region to the urban-type settlement of Staryi Saltiv, came under enemy fire. A 6-month-old child was injured in one of these cars. In addition, a 13-year-old girl went missing.
