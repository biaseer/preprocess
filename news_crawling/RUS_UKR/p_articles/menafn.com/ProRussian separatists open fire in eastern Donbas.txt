On Tuesday, Russian President Vladimir Putin declared the recognition of two eastern Ukrainian separatist territories as "independent" republics, as well as the deployment of Russian military to "maintain peace."
Pro-separatists deployed a variety of heavy weapons, including rockets, heavy artillery, anti-tank missiles, and mortars, according to the ministry.
MENAFN Pro-Russian separatists opened fire in the eastern Donbas region on Wednesday, killing one Ukrainian soldier and injuring six others, according to the country's Defense Ministry.
The ministry claimed in a statement that separatists in the region have violated 96 cease-fire agreements.
