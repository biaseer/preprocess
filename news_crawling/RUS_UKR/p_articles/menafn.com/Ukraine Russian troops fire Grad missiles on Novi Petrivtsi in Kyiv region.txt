MENAFN- UkrinForm Russian invaders fired Grad rockets on Novi Petrivtsi in Kyiv region. The Kyiv region police department wrote this on Facebook.
As Ukrinform reported, due to rising water in the Irpin River in Kyiv region, pontoon bridges built by Russian invaders to move along the river were demolished.
'Today, March 19, militants fired Grad rockets on Novi Petrivtsi. As a result of the shelling, the field caught fire and a filling station was damaged,' the report says.
