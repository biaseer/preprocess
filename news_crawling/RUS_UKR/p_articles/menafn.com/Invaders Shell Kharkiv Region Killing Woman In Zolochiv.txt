Invaders Shell Kharkiv Region Killing Woman In Zolochiv

MENAFN- UkrinForm A woman was killed and a man was wounded in Russian shelling in Kharkiv region. Oleh Synehubov, Head of the Kharkiv Regional Military Administration, wrote this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports.

Today, the occupiers used MLRS to shell the town of Zolochiv, Bohodukhiv district, and the village of Donets, Izium district. In Zolochiv, a projectile hit a residential building. A 52-year-old woman died,' Synehubov wrote.  Read also:  Enemy hit enterprise in  Kharkiv  with cluster munitions  mayor He added that a 49-year-old man was wounded in Donets.
