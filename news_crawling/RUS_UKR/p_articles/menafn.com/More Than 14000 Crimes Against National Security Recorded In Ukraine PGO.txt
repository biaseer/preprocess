MENAFN- UkrinForm A total of 14,134 crimes against national security were recorded in Ukraine since the Russian invasion started. According to the Prosecutor General's Office of Ukraine, 30,253 crimes of aggression and war crimes were registered, of which 1,069 were committed in the past week. The number of recorded crimes against national security stands at 14,134 541 in the past week.
The PGO also added that on the economic front in the past week, almost UAH 6 billion of assets were seized and handed over to the state.
The PGO clarified that 105 crimes related to treason were registered in the past week, some of the culprits have already been brought to justice.
Since the Russian invasion started, a total of 377 children have been killed and over 733 injured in Ukraine.
