Occupiers fire on Ukrainian positions near Vodiane
20220210
https://menafn.com/1103671734/Occupiers-fire-on-Ukrainian-positions-near-Vodiane&source=19
MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, February 9, the Russian-occupation troops violated the ceasefire in the Joint Forces Operation JFO area in eastern Ukraine once. Outside Vodiane, Ukrainian positions came under hand-held antitank grenade launcher and tripod-mounted man-portable antitank gun fire, the JFO press center posted on Facebook.
Ukrainian defenders returned fire without using weapons prohibited by the Minsk agreements and forced the enemy to cease shelling.
