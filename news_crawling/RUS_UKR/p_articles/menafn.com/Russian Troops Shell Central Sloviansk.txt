Russian Troops Shell Central Sloviansk
20220511

MENAFN- UkrinForm Russian troops have shelled the central part of Sloviansk, Donetsk Region. The damage rate is yet to be reported. The relevant statement was made by Sloviansk City Military and Civil Administration Head Vadym Liakh on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
