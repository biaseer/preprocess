Blinken visited Kyiv on January 19 and met with President Volodymyr Zelensky and Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba.
Kuleba added that at yesterday's meeting with Blinken, he 'reaffirmed his readiness for a trilateral meeting at the level of foreign ministers if Mr. Lavrov agrees to that.'
MENAFN- UkrinForm Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba has said he is ready for a trilateral meeting with U.S. Secretary of State Antony Blinken and Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov if the Russian side agrees to this meeting. He stated this at a virtual briefing on Thursday, January 20, Ukrinform reports.
