'Fighting continues in the Izium direction. Today, air defense mobile units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed a Russian Su-34 fighter-bomber in the Izium district. This is one of the newest aircraft of the enemy army,' the report says.
As Ukrinform reported, from February 24 to March 27, the Russian army lost about 16,600 people, 582 tanks, 1,664 armored personnel vehicles, 294 artillery systems, 93 MLRS, 52 anti-aircraft warfare systems, 121 aircraft, 127 helicopters, 1,144 vehicles, 7 shipsboats. Data are being updated. The calculation is complicated by the high intensity of hostilities.
