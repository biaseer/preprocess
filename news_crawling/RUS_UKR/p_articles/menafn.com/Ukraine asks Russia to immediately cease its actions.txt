MENAFN On Wednesday, Ukraine's Foreign Ministry asked that Russia "immediately cease its actions" in Kharkiv and Sumy and arrange for the evacuation of civilians, including foreign students, to "safer" Ukrainian towns.
It added that, "attempting to arrange evacuations through cities that are being subjected to Russian bombing and missile strikes is extremely dangerous."
According to the statement, Ukraine "stands ready" to assist international students in relocating from Kharkiv and Sumy "should Russia commit to a cease-fire."
