Ukrainian Forces Shoot Down Russias Su34 Over Kharkiv Region
20220523
https://menafn.com/1104239816/Ukrainian-Forces-Shoot-Down-Russias-Su-34-Over-Kharkiv-Region
MENAFN- UkrinForm The Ukrainian military have shot down Russia's Su-34 fighter-bomber aircraft near Kupiansk, Kharkiv Region. The relevant statement was made by Yurii Kochevenko, an officer of the 95th Separate Air Assault Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and May 18, 2022, Russia's total combat losses in the war against Ukraine reached about 28,300 troops.
