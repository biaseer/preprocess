MENAFN Due to the progressively escalating dispute between Russia and Ukraine the European financial market's leading indexes ended the day with losses on Monday.
Meanwhile, Italy's FTSE MIB fell by 550 points, or 2.04 percent, to close at 26,415 points.  France's CAC 40 plunged by approximately 160 points, or 2.27 percent, to reach 6,852 points by the end of the day.
The DAX 30 index in Germany fell by 311 points, or 2.02 percent, to end at 15,113. The FTSE 100 index in London plummeted by 129 points, or 1.69 percent, to hit 7,531.
