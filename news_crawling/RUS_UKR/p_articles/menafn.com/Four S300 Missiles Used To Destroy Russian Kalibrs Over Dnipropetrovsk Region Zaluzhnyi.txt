A reminder that, in the morning of August 20, 2022, the Ukrainian anti-aircraft defense systems shot down four enemy Kalibr-type cruise missiles over Dnipropetrovsk Region.
The relevant statement was made by Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Valerii Zaluzhnyi on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
MENAFN- UkrinForm The Ukrainian military have used just four S-300 system missiles to destroy four Kalibr-type cruise missiles fired by Russian troops at Dnipropetrovsk Region. The relevant statement was made by Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Valerii Zaluzhnyi on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
