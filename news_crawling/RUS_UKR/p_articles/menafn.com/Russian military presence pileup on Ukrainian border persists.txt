MENAFN According to the NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg, the Russian military presence pileup close to the Ukrainian remains persistent, and the potential of warfare is genuine.
In an assertion that happened at the Munich Security Conference, Jens Stoltenberg declared that, despite Moscow's claims, we have seen no sign of withdrawal or de-escalation so far.
He further asserted that, it is not too late for Russia to change course. To step back from the brink. Stop preparing for war. And start working for a peaceful solution.
