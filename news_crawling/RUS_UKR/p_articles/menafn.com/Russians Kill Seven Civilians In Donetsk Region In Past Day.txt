Russians Kill Seven Civilians In Donetsk Region In Past Day
20220812
https://menafn.com/1104687478/Russians-Kill-Seven-Civilians-In-Donetsk-Region-In-Past-Day
MENAFN- UkrinForm The Russian army killed seven civilians and injured 14 more in Donetsk region over the past day, August 11. 'On August 11, the Russians killed seven civilians of Donetsk region: three  in Bakhmut, two  in Kurakhove, one  in Marinka, and another one  in Avdiivka. Fourteen more people were injured,' Pavlo Kyrylenko, Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration, posted on Facebook .
