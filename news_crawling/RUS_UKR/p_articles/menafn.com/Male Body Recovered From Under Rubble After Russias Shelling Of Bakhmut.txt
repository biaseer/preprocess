Male Body Recovered From Under Rubble After Russias Shelling Of Bakhmut

MENAFN- UkrinForm A male body has been recovered from under the rubble in Bakhmut, Donetsk Region. The relevant statement was made by the Ukrainian State Emergency Service on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
So far, rescuers have revealed and recovered a male body from under the rubble. Two more persons may be remaining trapped. Rescue works continue.
At 10:25 a.m., the Main Department personnel launched rescue and salvage operations at the site of a two-storey railway repair depot in Bakhmut, which was destroyed in the enemy shelling last night, the report states.
