MENAFN- UkrinForm A total of 614 buildings were damaged and 90 destroyed completely by Russian troops in the village of Dymer, Kyiv Region. The relevant statement was made by Kyiv Regional Military Administration Head Oleksiy Kuleba on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
A reminder that, on February 24, 2022, Russian President Vladimir Putin initiated a full-scale invasion of Ukraine, starting a war. Russian troops are shelling and bombarding Ukrainian cities and villages, torturing and murdering civilians.
In the village of Dymer, due to the Russian aggression, 614 buildings were damaged, 90 buildings were destroyed completely. A plant of one of Ukraine's largest window manufacturers was also damaged, Kuleba wrote.
As part of his visit, Kuleba inspected the residential houses destroyed and discussed the top-priority tasks to rebuild settlements.
The relevant statement was made by Kyiv Regional Military Administration Head Oleksiy Kuleba on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
We are making every effort to ensure that local residents can return to their homes as soon as possible, and that the proper living conditions in communities are restored, Kuleba stressed.
