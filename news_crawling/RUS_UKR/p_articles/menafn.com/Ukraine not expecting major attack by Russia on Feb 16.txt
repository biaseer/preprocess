MENAFN According to the secretary of the National Defense and Security Council, Ukraine does not expect Russia to start a major invasion on the nation.
Oleksiy Danilov updated the Ukrainian journalists that they are awake of changes and dangers on their land.
Danilov stated they do not expect an invasion from Russia on February 16 or 17, underlining that the condition is totally controlled.
But, he emphasized that Ukraine is as well ready to any invasion, saying that Russia may start attacks.
US Leader Joe Biden before updated the presidents of Western nations that Russia could invade Ukraine on February 16.
