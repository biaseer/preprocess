A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and March 30, 2022, Russia lost about 17.3 thousand troops in the war against Ukraine. The enemy's combat losses also included 605 tanks, 1,723 armored fighting vehicles, 305 artillery systems, 96 multiple launch rocket systems, 54 anti-aircraft warfare systems, 131 aircrafts, 131 helicopters, 1,184 motor vehicles, seven boatscutters, 75 fuel tanks, 81 unmanned aerial vehicles, 21 special equipment units and four mobile SRBM systems.
In particular, Ukrainian defenders destroyed four Russian planes, one unmanned aerial vehicle and two cruise missiles.
The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, the Air Force of the Armed Forces of Ukraine have destroyed seven enemy air targets. The relevant statement was made by the Air Force Command of the Armed Forces of Ukraine on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
