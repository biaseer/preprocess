On July 25, 2022, an 8-year-old girl was injured in Russia's shelling of Mykolaiv District, Mykolaiv Region.
A total of 2,188 educational institutions were damaged across Ukraine as a result of Russia's daily air and artillery strikes, and 221 of them were destroyed completely.
The Ukrainian side also received information about a 17-year-old boy injured in Russia's shelling of Donetsk Region's Hirnyk on July 18, 2022.
As of July 25, 2022, the number of those killed remained unchanged 358 children. The number of those injured exceeded 686 children, the report states.
Currently, most casualties were recorded in Donetsk Region 362, Kharkiv Region 194, Kyiv Region 116, Chernihiv Region 68, Luhansk Region 61, Mykolaiv Region 55, Kherson Region 54 and Zaporizhzhia Region 40.
These data are not final, as they are being investigated within the areas of hostilities, temporarily occupied and liberated areas.
