The Yale School of Management has published a list of corporations that are leaving Russia.
Amongst the firms that have announced their withdrawal are Airbnb, Deloitte, eBay, Netflix, Shell, Uber, Renault, Samsung, Apple, TikTok, Toyota, Visa, as well as Adidas.
The corporations are divided into five categories: withdrawal 172, suspension 195, trimming back 31, buying time 56 enterprises deferring future assets, and drilling in 43 companies defying demands for exit.
Professor Jeffrey Sonnenfeld, who is the representative of Yales management, stated that the list helped galvanize millions around the world to raise awareness and take action.
