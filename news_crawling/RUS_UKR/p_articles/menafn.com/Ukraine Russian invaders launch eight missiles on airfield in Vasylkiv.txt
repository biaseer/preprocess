MENAFN- UkrinForm The enemy has launched eight missiles on an airfield in the city of Vasylkiv, Kyiv Region. The relevant statement was made by Vasylkiv Mayor Natalia Balasynovych on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
In her words, the airport's runway was destroyed. One missile hit an ammunition depot, and shells are now exploding. A warehouse with fuels and oils was also destroyed.
