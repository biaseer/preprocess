Ukraine  Air defense units down enemy helicopter in Kyiv region
20220317
https://menafn.com/1103869647/Ukraine-Air-defense-units-down-enemy-helicopter-in-Kyiv-region&source=138
MENAFN- UkrinForm Ukrainian military shot down a KA-52 helicopter of Russian invaders in Bucha district of Kyiv region. 'At 11:45, two enemy helicopters were spotted entering the impact zone of Ukrainian troops. The air defense units of our Land Forces damaged one enemy helicopter,' the Kyiv Regional Military Administration posted on Telegram.
As reported, on the night of March 17, an enemy SU-25 plane was destroyed and a SU-35 plane was shot down in the sky over Kyiv region.
'At 11:45, two enemy helicopters were spotted entering the impact zone of Ukrainian troops. The air defense units of our Land Forces damaged one enemy helicopter,' the Kyiv Regional Military Administration posted on Telegram.
