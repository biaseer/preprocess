The SBU initiated the seizure of Rosnefts assets in Ukraine... According to the materials of the SBU, the activities of the energy company, which was part of the Russian Rosneft group, were blocked, the message states.
MENAFN The Security Service of Ukraine SBU announced on its Telegram channel on Saturday, May 28th, that it has begun processes to take the resources of Russian oil company Rosneft.
According to the SBU notification, about 20 million hryvnia $677,000 in assets reportedly belonging to the Rosneft subsidiary in Ukraine were banned, and the company's electrical license was also halted.
