Since the battle began on Feb. 24, Ukrainian troops have damaged 200 Russian planes, 164 helicopters, 416 unmanned aerial vehicles, 1,220 tanks, and 2,958 armored vehicles, according to the report.
Based on the report, Russia has lost 195 multiple rocket launcher systems, 2,087 vehicles, 95 cruise missiles, 89 anti-aircraft systems, and 13 vessels.
According to United Nation figures, 3,573 people have murdered and 3,816 have been wounded since Russia commenced its assault on Ukraine. The real cost is expected to be far greater.
According to the Ukrainian General Staff, at least 200 additional Russian soldiers have been murdered in the last day.
MENAFN The Ukrainian military reported on Sunday that 27,400 Russian troops had been murdered so far in the conflict in Ukraine.
