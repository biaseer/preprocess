MENAFN- UkrinForm Since the beginning of the war against Ukraine, 31 battalion tactical groups BTGs of Russian invading troops have lost their combat capability. 'As of now, 18 battalion tactical groups have completely lost their combat capability, 13 have been completely destroyed. Thirty-one battalion tactical groups have actually lost their combat capability over two weeks. These are terrible losses that the Russians did not suffer previously,' Head of the Chief Directorate of Intelligence of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Kyrylo Budanov said in an interview, Ukrinform reports.
