A 25-storey residential building in Shevchenkivskyi district was partially destroyed as a result of the enemy strike. Ten people were injured. Four of them were hospitalized.
MENAFN- UkrinForm In Kyiv, rescuers found a body under the rubble of a high-rise building hit by the Russian military on the evening of April 28. 'Rescuers, who continue to inspect and clear away the rubble of the residential building in Shevchenkivskyi district hit yesterday, have just found the dead body,' Kyiv Mayor Vitaliy Klitschko posted on Telegram .
As reported, on April 28, the Russians again fired missiles on the capital of Ukraine.
