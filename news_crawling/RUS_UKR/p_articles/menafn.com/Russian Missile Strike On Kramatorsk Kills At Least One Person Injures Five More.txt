Russian Missile Strike On Kramatorsk Kills At Least One Person Injures Five More

MENAFN- UkrinForm At least one person was killed in a Russian missile strike on Kramatorsk city in Donetsk region, the number of those injured grew to five. Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration Pavlo Kyrylenko wrote on Telegram, Ukrinform reports.

As Ukrinform reported, earlier it was stated that three people were injured in a Russian missile strike on Kramatorsk.
'As of now, it is known about one dead person and five wounded. Two private houses were completely destroyed, another 21 were damaged. These numbers can still change,' Kyrylenko wrote.
He emphasized that it is dangerous to remain in Donetsk region and called on citizens to evacuate.
