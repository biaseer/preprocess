MENAFN- UkrinFormOver the past day, the occupiers launched five attacks on the positions of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the Joint Forces Operation JFO area in eastern Ukraine. Over the past day, November 18, five ceasefire violations by Russian-occupation troops, including two with the use of the weapons banned under the Minsk agreements, were recorded, the press center of the JFO Headquarters informs.
In the area of Pivdenne 40km north-east of Donetsk, Ukrainian positions came under heavy machine gun fire.
Outside New York 35km north of Donetsk, the enemy fired grenade launchers of different systems and heavy machine guns.
