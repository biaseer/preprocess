MENAFN The US government is rushing the delivery of five transport helicopters to Kiev, fearing that Moscow is about to "invade" Ukraine. The Mi-17 helicopters were bought from Russia and were initially meant for Afghanistan's former government which was supported by Westerns however, it retreated to Taliban in August 2021.
The helicopters are already in Ukraine, where they were being maintained as per the Pentagon orders. Moreover, they were scheduled to be transferred to Afghanistan up until Taliban took over. According to Foreign Policy, Ukrainian Defense Minister, Alexey Reznikov asked for the Pentagon for them in November 2021, alongside ammunition intended for the -now-destroyed Afghan Army.
