MENAFN Turkey declined Russia's determination to recognize Ukraine's separate areas of Donetsk and Luhansk, and described it as unacceptable.
The decision was following Russian Leader Vladimir Putin officially labeled Ukraine's Donetsk and Luhansk areas as independent states and agreed with their nationalist presidents in the Kremlin.MENAFN23022022000045012476ID1103750041
According to the Turkish Foreign Ministry "The decision of the Russian Federation to recognize the so-called Donetsk and Luhansk Republics, in addition to contradicting the Minsk Agreements, constitutes a clear violation of Ukraine's political unity and territorial integrity," noting "The Russian Federations decision is unacceptable and we reject it."
