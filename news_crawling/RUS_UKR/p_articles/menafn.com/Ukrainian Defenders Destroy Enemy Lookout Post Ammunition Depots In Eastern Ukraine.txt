Ukrainian defenders continue to hold back the aggressor within the control area of the East Operational Tactical Group. On June 7, 2022, the Ukrainian military destroyed a lookout post and two ammunition depots of Russian fascist forces, the report states.
A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and June 7, 2022, Russia's total combat losses in Ukraine reached about 31,360 troops.
MENAFN- UkrinForm The Ukrainian military have destroyed a Russian lookout post and two ammunition depots in eastern Ukraine. The relevant statement was made by the East Operational Tactical Group on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
The enemy's losses included 56 troops, one mortar, 12 artillery trucks, one fuel tank and one special equipment unit.
