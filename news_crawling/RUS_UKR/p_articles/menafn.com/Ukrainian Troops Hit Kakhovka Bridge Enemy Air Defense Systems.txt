MENAFN- UkrinForm On August 24, Ukrainian troops hit the Kakhovka Bridge, Russian air defense systems, a command and observation post, and an ammunition depot. 'Our aircraft launched four strikes on areas where the enemy's manpower, weapons, and equipment were amassed in Beryslav district, in particular, the air defense systems in Nova Kakhovka,' the Operational Command 'South' posted on Facebook .
The Kakhovka Bridge was attacked by the Ukrainian missile and artillery units as part of fire missions.
'A command and observation point of the battalion tactical group of the 33rd motorized rifle regiment and the 7th military base was hit,' the Operational Command 'South' added.
