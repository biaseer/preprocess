MENAFN- UkrinForm Russian troops continue to shell border areas in the Chernihiv region. According to Ukrinform, the Operational Command North said this on Facebook .
'Today, at about 15:45, observers recorded six shells from Russian territory, probably fired from an automatic grenade launcher, in the area of the settlement of Senkivka,' the statement reads.
On May 21, Russian troops shelled the area between the Kharkiv and Sumy regions and the border areas of the Chernihiv region.
