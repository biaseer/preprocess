MENAFN- UkrinForm On Thursday, during the air raid siren, an aircraft of the Armed Forces of Ukraine shot down a Russian cruise missile over Romny District, Sumy Region.
The relevant statement was made by Sumy Regional Military Administration Head Dmytro Zhyvytskyi on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
