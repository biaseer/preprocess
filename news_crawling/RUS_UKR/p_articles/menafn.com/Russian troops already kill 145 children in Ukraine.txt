MENAFN- UkrinForm A total of 145 children were killed and 222 were wounded in Ukraine as a result of full-scale hostilities by Russian troops. According to Ukrinform, the Ukrainian Prosecutor General's Office said this in a statement released on Telegram .

Russian bombing and shelling damaged 790 educational institutions. Seventy-five of them were completely destroyed. The situation is the worst in the Donetsk, Kharkiv, Mykolaiv, Sumy, Kyiv, Kherson, Chernihiv regions and the city of Kyiv.
The greatest number of children affected by the war was recorded in the Kyiv region - 69, the Kharkiv region - 49, the Donetsk region - 54, the Chernihiv region - 39, the Mykolaiv region - 30, the Luhansk region - 28, the Zaporizhia region - 22, the Kherson region - 25, the Ukrainian capital - 16, the Sumy region - 16, the Zhytomyr region - 15.
