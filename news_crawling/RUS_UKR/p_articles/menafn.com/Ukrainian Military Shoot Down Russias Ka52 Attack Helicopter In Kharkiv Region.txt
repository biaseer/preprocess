MENAFN- UkrinForm In Kharkiv Region, Ukrainian servicemen have shot down Russia's Ka-52 'Alligator' attack helicopter. The relevant statement was made by the 93rd Separate Kholodnyi Yar Mechanized Brigade on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
This afternoon Kholodnyi Yar's anti-aircraft missile battalion has destroyed Russia's Ka-52 attack helicopter in the Izium direction, Kharkiv Region, the report states.
A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and June 13, 2022, Russia's total combat losses in Ukraine reached about 32,300 troops.
