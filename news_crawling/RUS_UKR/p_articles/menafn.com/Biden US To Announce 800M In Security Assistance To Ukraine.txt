MENAFN- UkrinForm U.S. President Joe Biden has announced that the United States will provide a new package of security assistance to Ukraine worth $800 million in the coming days, which will include, in particular, new air defense systems, artillery systems, and ammunition. 'In the next few days, we intend to announce more than $800 million more,' The U.S. President said at a press conference in Madrid following the NATO summit, an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
