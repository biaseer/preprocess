In the morning of May 16, 2022, a child was injured in Russia's missile strike on Odesa Region, which was launched with Russia's strategic aviation. A tourist infrastructure facility was hit, buildings destroyed, and a fire broke out, the report states.
The relevant statement was made by the South Operational Command on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
MENAFN- UkrinForm On May 16, 2022, Russian invaders launched a missile strike on Odesa Region. The missile fired from an enemy aircraft hit a tourist infrastructure facility not far from Odesa, leaving two adults and a child injured. The relevant statement was made by the South Operational Command on Telegram , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
