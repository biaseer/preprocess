MENAFN- UkrinForm The Russians launched another air strike on Kharkiv. A five-storey residential building on Novobavarskyi Avenue has been partially destroyed. As Ukrinform reported earlier, the State Emergency Service of Ukraine reported the news on Facebook .
The head of the Novobavarskyi district, Tetiana Tsybulnyk, said there were people killed and many injured. People are trapped under the rubble.
