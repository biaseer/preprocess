A Ukrainian soldier was wounded in the enemy shelling. He was taken to a medical facility, his health status is satisfactory.
Russian mercenaries fired small arms on Ukrainian positions near Pivdenne 40km north-east of Donetsk, Vodiane 94km south of Donetsk, Nevelske 18km north-west of Donetsk. Two attacks from small arms were launched on the Ukrainian defenders of Pisky 11km north-west of Donetsk, the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine informs.

MENAFN- UkrinForm Over the past day, February 7, the Russian-occupation troops violated the ceasefire in the Joint Forces Operation JFO area in eastern Ukraine five times. Russian mercenaries fired small arms on Ukrainian positions near Pivdenne 40km north-east of Donetsk, Vodiane 94km south of Donetsk, Nevelske 18km north-west of Donetsk. Two attacks from small arms were launched on the Ukrainian defenders of Pisky 11km north-west of Donetsk, the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine informs.
