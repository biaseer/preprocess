'In the past 24 hours, the air defense units of the airborne assault forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine shot down two Russian unmanned aerial vehicles of various types: a multi-purpose unmanned helicopter Horizon Air S-100 and a multi-purpose unmanned aerial vehicle complex Orlan-10. They were shot down by Stinger and Starstreak portable air defense systems, the report says.
As reported, between February 24 and April 20, 2022, Russia's total combat losses in Ukraine reached about 20,900 troops.
MENAFN- UkrinForm Ukrainian paratroopers destroyed two Russian drones Horizon Air S-100 and Orlan-10 using Stinger and Starstreak portable air defense missile systems. The Command of the Assault Troops of the Armed Forces of Ukraine said this in a Facebook post, Ukrinform reports.
