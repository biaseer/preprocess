MENAFN- UkrinForm Last night Russian invaders opened fire on the outskirts of Donetsk Region's Kramatorsk with multiple launch rocket systems MLRS. Destructions were reported but, fortunately, there were no casualties among civilians. The relevant statement was made by Kramatorsk Mayor Oleksandr Honcharenko on Facebook , an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
