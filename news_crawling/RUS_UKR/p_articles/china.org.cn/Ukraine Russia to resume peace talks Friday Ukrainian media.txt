MOSCOWKIEV, April 1 Xinhua -- Ukraine and Russia will resume their peace talks on Friday in an online format, the Ukrinform news agency reported Thursday, citing a member of the Ukrainian delegation David Arakhamia.
According to the TASS news agency, Kremlin Spokesman Dmitry Peskov said on Thursday that "as for the resumption of the peace talks tomorrow ... if it happens, we will inform you."
