New Zealand applies new sanctions against Russia
20220502
https://www.teletrader.com/new-zealand-applies-new-sanctions-against-russia/news/details/57792167
New Zealand announced that it imposed new sanctions against Russian senators and companies, New Zealand Foreign Minister Nanai Mahuta said on Monday.
According to the statement released by the government, the latest round of sanctions targets 170 members of Russia's Parliament. In addition, the measures are also imposed against six companies and organizations in the defense sector, the official release detailed.
Earlier, New Zealand issued sanctions against Russian President Vladimir Putin. Besides, the country placed a 35 tariff on all imports from Russia in an effort to pressure Moscow over its actions in Ukraine.
