The statement follows the announcement by the Russian Foreign Ministry, stating that the meeting between the two leaders is currently being arranged.
In a possible meeting between Russian President Vladimir Putin and his American counterpart Joe Biden, Ukraine will undoubtedly be addressed, Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov said on Monday.
"In the course of the next contact between the presidents, whose date and time are still being discussed, undoubtedly, the topic of Ukraine will arise one way or another," Lavrov said at a press conference.
