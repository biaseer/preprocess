The third man, identified as Alexander Tsilyk, an operative of the Main Intelligence Directorate of Ukraine's Defense Ministry, was allegedly sent to Russia "to commit a terrorist attack."
The Russian Federal Security Service FSB said on Thursday it detained three people suspected of working for Ukrainian intelligence services.
According to the FSB, two of the detained men, Zinoviy Koval and his son Igor, are agents of the Security Service of Ukraine SBU who came to Russia to collect "information and take photos and videos of strategically important enterprises and transportation infrastructure."
