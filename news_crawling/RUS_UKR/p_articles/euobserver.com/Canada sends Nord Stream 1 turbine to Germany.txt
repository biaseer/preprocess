Canada sends Nord Stream 1 turbine to Germany

Canada on Sunday sent a repairedturbineof theNord Stream1pipeline to Germanyby plane, Reuters reported on Monday. Russian energy giant Gazprom had asked Germany for the repaired turbine to restart gas flows, but it may still take five to seven days to reach Russia, according to the Russian newspaper Kommersant. Canada was slammed by Ukraine for granting the turbine an exemption from sanctions imposed on Russia.
