Putin proposes to create state information protection system

According to the Head of the State, it is necessary to strengthen the defence of the home digital space. There should not be risks of the confidential information and personal data leaks.
Putin believes the information protection tools must be modified as the development of Russian economical and social areas as well as national defence capability strictly depend on them.
MOSCOW, June 3 RAPSI - Russian President Vladimir Putin has proposed to consider creation of the state information protection system.
Digital processes actively running in the system of Russias economy and public administration should be in maximum protected from any possible negative impact from outside, Russias Presidents stated.
