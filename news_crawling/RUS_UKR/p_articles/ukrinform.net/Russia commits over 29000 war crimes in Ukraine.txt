The number of registered crimes of aggression and war crimes, committed by the Russian Federation, reached 29,194 1,146 over the last week.
According to the PGO, as a result of Russian aggression in Ukraine, 362 children were killed and 716  injured.
As reported by Ukrinform, the European Union provided information to the UN International Court of Justice in The Hague as part of the case in which Ukraine accuses the Russian Federation of plotting acts of genocide amid the war.
