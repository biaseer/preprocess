Russia strikes infrastructure of Zhytomyr region Air defense units down four missiles
20220523
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-ato/3489645-russia-strikes-infrastructure-of-zhytomyr-region-air-defense-units-down-four-missiles.html
According to available information, Zhytomyr region was attacked from the south-eastern direction by sea-launched cruise missiles.
It is noted that three missiles were destroyed by aircraft, and another one  by a unit of anti-aircraft missile forces.
Today, May 22, at about 13:00, the enemy launched a missile strike on the infrastructure of Zhytomyr region. The air defense units of the Air Command Center downed four cruise missiles, the Air Command Center posted on Facebook, Ukrinform reports.
As reported, the Air Defense Forces of Ukraine shot down a Russian missile over Fastiv district of Kyiv region on Sunday. According to preliminary information, there are no casualties or damage.
