Russian occupiers continue to conduct hostilities along the captured defense lines. The composition and location of enemy units remained unchanged.
In addition, the Armed Forces of Ukraine destroyed Russias self-propelled howitzer Msta-S and three motor vehicles. An enemy ammunition depot in Novovoskresensk, Kherson Region, was struck. The enemys final losses are yet to be checked.
On August 23, 2022, Russian troops attacked the positions of Ukrainian forces with assault aircrafts and helicopters 10 times.
Russias naval group in the Black Sea along the Crimean coast also reduced to 10 maneuvering vessels. There are four combat-ready missile carriers, including one submarine, with a total of 28 Kalibr-type cruise missiles.
The relevant statement was made by the South Operational Command on Facebook, an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
