Verkhovna Rada thanks partners for assistance in countering Russian aggression
20220201
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-polytics/3395620-verkhovna-rada-thanks-partners-for-assistance-in-countering-russian-aggression.html
As an Ukrinform correspondent reports, several dozen MPs from various parliamentary factions and groups came to the rostrum. They expressed gratitude to international partners for assistance in countering the military aggression of the Russian Federation. In particular, the Ukrainian parliamentarians unfolded the flags of the United States, Canada, Great Britain, and Poland.
As reported, the prime ministers of Poland, the Netherlands, and the United Kingdom arrive in Kyiv on February 1-2.
