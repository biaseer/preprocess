Ukraine Army destroys 12 enemy tanks and one helicopter in JFO area
20220510
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-ato/3480163-ukraine-army-destroys-12-enemy-tanks-and-one-helicopter-in-jfo-area.html
In particular, Ukrainian defenders destroyed 12 enemy tanks, four artillery systems, 12 armored fighting vehicles, two special engineering vehicles and seven motor vehicles.
A reminder that, between February 24, 2022 and May 10, 2022, Russias total combat losses in Ukraine reached about 26,000 troops.
The Ukrainian anti-aircraft defense units also shot down Russias Mi-24 helicopter and three Orlan-10 unmanned aerial vehicles UAVs over Donetsk Region and Luhansk Region.
The relevant statement was made by the JFO Groups press service on Facebook, an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
On May 10, 2022, the JFO Group successfully repelled 12 enemy attacks. Thanks to the skilful actions and competence of the Ukrainian military, Russian occupiers suffered losses, the report states.
