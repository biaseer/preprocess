Ukraine forces shoot down Russian Su25 frogfoot attack aircraft in Donetsk region
20220729
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-ato/3540002-ukraine-forces-shoot-down-russian-su25-frogfoot-attack-aircraft-in-donetsk-region.html
As Ukrinform reported earlier, another Russian Su-25 attack aircraft was downed by the same Ukrainian unit on July 24.
It is noted that with the onset of the large-scale armed aggression of the Russian forces against Ukraine, the air defense units of the 25th Separate Airborne Brigade shot down 29 enemy means of aerial attack, including 15 warplanes, seven helicopters, and seven UAVs.
"Yesterday, July 29, in Donetsk region, a serviceman with the air defense unit of the 25th Separate Airborne Brigade of the Sicheslav Airborne Assault Division shot down with an Igla MANPADS system another Su-25 armored attack aircraft operated by the Russian occupiers," the report reads.
