Over 22 tonnes of humanitarian cargo delivered to occupied territories of eastern Ukraine
20220127
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-society/3392886-over-22-tonnes-of-humanitarian-cargo-delivered-to-occupied-territories-of-eastern-ukraine.html
As Ukrinform reported, on December 22, humanitarian cargo weighing over nine tonnes was delivered to Russia-occupied parts of Donetsk and Luhansk regions.
The cargo was transferred from the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees.
Today, January 27, humanitarian cargo weighing a total of 22.5 tonnes has been delivered to Russia-occupied parts of Donetsk and Luhansk regions," the report says.
It is noted that one truck carrying humanitarian aid crossed the Novotroitske entry-exit checkpoint, according to the ministry.
