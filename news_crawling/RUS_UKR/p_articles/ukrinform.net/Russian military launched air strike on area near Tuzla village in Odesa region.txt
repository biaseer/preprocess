Russian military launched air strike on area near Tuzla village in Odesa region
20220307
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-ato/3422372-russian-military-launched-air-strike-on-area-near-tuzla-village-in-odesa-region.html
"The enemy fired a missile from the sea on the area near the village of Tuzla... The strike was aimed at critical infrastructure," Bratchuk noted.
According to him, information on neighboring settlements with Tuzla is being clarified. No casualties have been reported.
Ukraine officially filed a lawsuit against the Russian Federation with the UN International Court of Justice in The Hague.
Spokesman of the operational headquarters of the Odesa Regional Military Administration Serhiy Bratchuk wrote this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports.
As reported, on February 24, Russian President Vladimir Putin declared war on Ukraine and launched a large-scale invasion. Russian troops have been shelling and destroying key infrastructure. Missiles hit residential buildings.
