Four civilians killed in Russias shelling of Donetsk Region over past day
20220602
https://www.ukrinform.net/rubric-ato/3497748-four-civilians-killed-in-russias-shelling-of-donetsk-region-over-past-day.html
On June 1, 2022, Russians killed four civilians in Donetsk Region, namely in such settlements as Soledar, New York, Tetianivka and Lyman. Nine more civilians were injured, Kyrylenko wrote.
According to the infographics posted by Kyrylenko, a total of 461 civilians have been killed and 1,202 injured in Donetsk Region since the Russian invasion started.
Three more people were reported killed in Russias shelling of Sviatohirsk Lavra. In Kostiantynivka, one more person was registered, who had been injured in Luhansk Region.
The relevant statement was made by Donetsk Regional Military Administration Head Pavlo Kyrylenko on Telegram, an Ukrinform correspondent reports.
At the moment, it is impossible to calculate the exact number of casualties in Mariupol and Volnovakha.
