Putin and Scholz discussed the results of the third round of talks between Russia and Ukraine
20220309
https://www.tellerreport.com/news/2022-03-09-putin-and-scholz-discussed-the-results-of-the-third-round-of-talks-between-russia-and-ukraine.HyeWrfLUZ5.html
Russian President Vladimir Putin and German Chancellor Olaf Scholz, during a discussion of a special operation to protect Donbass, discussed options for political and diplomatic efforts, in particular, the results of the third round of negotiations between Russia and Ukraine.
Earlier in Kyiv, they said that they turned to Scholz with a request to organize a meeting between Russian and Ukrainian presidents Vladimir Putin and Vladimir Zelensky.
They also paid special attention to the humanitarian aspects of the situation in Ukraine and in the Donetsk and Lugansk People's Republics.
