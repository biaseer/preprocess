The Russian Guard detained employees of the Zaporizhzhya NPP who collaborated with the Armed Forces of Ukraine
20220824

It is specified that a violator of access control, an accomplice of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, who transmitted the coordinates of the movement of columns of Russian equipment, was also detained.
The servicemen of the Russian Guard stopped illegal actions that threatened the safety of the Zaporozhye nuclear power plant - during special targeted events, they detained two employees of the nuclear power plant, who transmitted information to the Armed Forces of Ukraine about the location of personnel and equipment on the territory of the plant itself, the department noted.
The Russian Guard detained two employees of the Zaporizhzhya NPP who collaborated with the Ukrainian military.
The National Guard added that in difficult conditions, the employees of the department continue to perform tasks for the reliable protection and defense of nuclear power plants.
On August 15, it was reported that employees of the Russian Guard during the week detained more than 50 people in the Kherson and Zaporozhye regions involved in the activities of nationalist organizations, the SBU and the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
