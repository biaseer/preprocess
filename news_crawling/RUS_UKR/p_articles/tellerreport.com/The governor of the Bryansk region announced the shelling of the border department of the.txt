The governor of the Bryansk region announced the shelling of the border department of the FSB

The governor of the Bryansk region, Alexander Bogomaz, announced the shelling of the border department of the FSB in the village of Belaya Berezka from Ukraine.
On April 29, a branch of the border department of the FSB of Russia in the Bryansk region was subjected to mortar fire from the territory of Ukraine in the village of Belaya Beryozka, Trubchevsky district, he wrote on Telegram.
As a result of the shelling, no one was injured, the water and electricity networks were damaged.
Earlier, the governor of the Kursk region, Roman Starovoit, announced the shelling of the village of Krupets from mortars.
