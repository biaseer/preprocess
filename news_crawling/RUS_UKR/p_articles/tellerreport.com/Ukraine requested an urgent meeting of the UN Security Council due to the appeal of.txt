Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmitry Kuleba announced an urgent request from the country to hold a meeting of the UN Security Council in connection with the appeal of the Donetsk and Luhansk People's Republics to Russia with a request to provide them with military assistance.
Ukraine has initiated an urgent meeting of the UN Security Council in connection with the appeal of the Russian occupation administrations in Donetsk and Luhansk to Russia with a request for military support, he wrote on his Twitter.
Ukraine initiated a meeting for the sake of UN security in connection with the Russian occupation administrations in Donetsk and Lugansk to Russia with a request for a military support, which is further escalating security.
Earlier, the heads of the Donetsk and Lugansk People's Republics Denis Pushilin and Leonid Pasechnik appealed to Russian President Vladimir Putin with a request to assist in repelling aggression from the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
