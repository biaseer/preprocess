Russian Ambassador to Washington Anatoly Antonov expressed hope that the US authorities would not supply multiple launch rocket systems to Ukraine.
According to Pentagon spokesman John Kirby, an official decision on the supply of multiple launch rocket systems to Kyiv has not yet been made.
US President Joe Biden said that the United States will not supply Ukraine with missile systems capable of delivering strikes on Russian territory.
The New York Times, citing sources, wrote that the administration of US President Joe Biden approved the decision to send multiple launch rocket systems MLRS to Ukraine.
