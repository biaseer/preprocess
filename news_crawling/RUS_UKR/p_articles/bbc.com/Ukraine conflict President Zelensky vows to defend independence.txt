Ukraine conflict President Zelensky vows to defend independence

Ukraine's President Volodymyr Zelensky has vowed to defend his country's independence in the face of the Russian invasion. He and his advisers are seen on the streets of Kyiv in a bid to show solidarity. The video was posted to President Zelensky's Facebook page on Friday evening.
