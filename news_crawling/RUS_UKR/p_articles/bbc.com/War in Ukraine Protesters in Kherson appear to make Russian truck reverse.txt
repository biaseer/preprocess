War in Ukraine Protesters in Kherson appear to make Russian truck reverse
20220321
https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-europe-60819031
Footage posted online appears to show Ukrainian civilians in Svobody Square, Kherson making a Russian truck reverse away from their protest on Sunday.
The reversing vehicle was marked with the letter "Z", which has become a symbol of support for Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
