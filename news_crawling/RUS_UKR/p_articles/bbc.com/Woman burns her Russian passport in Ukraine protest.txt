Woman burns her Russian passport in Ukraine protest
20220302
https://www.bbc.com/news/av/uk-scotland-60589079
Anna Jakubova used a lighter to ignite and burn the passport in front of hundreds of people gathered in Edinburgh.
A woman has set fire to her Russian passport as part of a protest over the invasion of Ukraine.
Ms Jakubova, 26, from Moscow, said she was horrified by the slaughter of civilians and ashamed to be Russian.
