The fire at the oil depot in Rivne region, which came under rocket fire on the evening of March the 28th, is still being extinguished.
In the Kharkiv region, the Russian army continues to use planes and artillery to shell villages, trying to capture the city of Izium.
The first round of talks between Ukraine and the Russian Federation took place in Istanbul today. The Ukrainian delegation stated that it had proposed a new system of security guarantees
Russian troops are concentrating their efforts on capturing Popasna. The head of the Luhansk military administration Serhii Haidai reported about it. During this day, the Russian army launched air strikes in the areas of Voievodivka, Rubizhne, Lysychansk, Kreminna, Zolote and Popasna.
