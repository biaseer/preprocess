Ukrainian parliament backs bill to seize Russiaowned assets in Ukraine
20220303
https://wsau.com/2022/03/03/ukrainian-parliament-backs-bill-to-seize-russia-owned-assets-in-ukraine/
LVIV Reuters      Ukraines parliament on Thursday approved a bill to allow the seizure of assets or property in Ukraine owned by Russia or Russian citizens due to the invasion of Ukraine.
Under the law, the government can suggest which assets to confiscate to the Security Council, which must then give its approval for their transfer to state ownership.
