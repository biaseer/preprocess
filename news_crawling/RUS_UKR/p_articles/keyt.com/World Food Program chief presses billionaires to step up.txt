World Food Program chief presses billionaires to step up
20220523
https://keyt.com/news/2022/05/23/world-food-program-chief-presses-billionaires-to-step-up/
DAVOS, Switzerland AP  The head of the U.N.s World Food Program is telling billionaires its time to step up as the global threat of food insecurity rises because of Russias war in Ukraine. Agency Executive Director David Beasley told The Associated Press at the World Economic Forum gathering on Monday that hes seen encouraging signs from some of the worlds richest people, like Elon Musk and Jeff Bezos. Musk and Bezos didnt immediately respond to emails or other messages seeking comment. Beasleys challenge came as a new study projected that nearly 1.9 billion people could face insecurity by November.
