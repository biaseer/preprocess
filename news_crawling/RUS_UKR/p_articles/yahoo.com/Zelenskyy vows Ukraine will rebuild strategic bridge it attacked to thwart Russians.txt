Zelenskyy vows Ukraine will rebuild strategic bridge it attacked to thwart Russians
20220728
https://news.yahoo.com/zelenskyy-vows-ukraine-rebuild-strategic-100127007.html
Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy on Wednesday vowed his country will rebuild a strategic bridge that his troops knocked out earlier, which was used by Russia to supply its forces in southern Ukraine's occupied Kherson region.
