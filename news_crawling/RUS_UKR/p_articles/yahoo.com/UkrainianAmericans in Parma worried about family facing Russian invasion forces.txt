UkrainianAmericans in Parma worried about family facing Russian invasion forces

Parma is home to the highest concentration of Ukrainian-Americans in Ohio, many of whom were up late Wednesday night anxiously watching the news coverage of Russias invasion, worried about friends and family still living in the besieged European nation.
