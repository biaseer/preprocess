President Biden held a nearly two-hour call with Chinese President Xi Jinping on Friday, warning him that there would be consequences if China provides material support to Russia. Biden will be traveling to Brussels on Wednesday to meet with allies as the situation in Ukraine worsens. NBCs Ali Vitali reports for Saturday TODAY.
