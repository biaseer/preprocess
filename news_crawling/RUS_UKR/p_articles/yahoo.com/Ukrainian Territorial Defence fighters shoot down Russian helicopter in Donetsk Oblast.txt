On 16 June, Ukrainian Territorial Defence fighters shot down a Russian Mi-35M helicopter in Donetsk Oblast.
Details: The General Staff has reported some successes for the Ukrainian Armed Forces in Zaporizhzhia Oblast. The Russian occupation army has suffered significant losses in personnel in Kherson Oblast.
