Russians launch missile attack on infrastructure in Odesa Oblast
20220822
https://news.yahoo.com/russians-launch-missile-attack-infrastructure-115900662.html
The Kh-59 air-launched cruise missiles were fired from Su-35 aircraft flying over the Black Sea.
On the night of Aug. 21, Russia fired five missiles at Odessa Oblast. Two of them were shot down by air defense forces, and three more hit the territory of an agricultural facility.
