The aircraft of the Air Force once again strikes at the positions of the occupying forces, Air Force Command reported.
At about 8 p.m. on May 25, from the territory of the Russian Federation, Tu-22M long-range bombers carried out missile strikes against Ukrainian positions in the Donetsk and Luhansk directions. Another strike was launched against Ukraines southern front.
Air Force Command also noted that while Russian aviation is growing around Ukraines borders, its aircraft is avoiding entering airspace covered by Ukraines air defense.
