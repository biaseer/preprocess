Russia says checkpoint in Kursk region shelled from Ukraine
20220430
https://news.yahoo.com/russia-says-checkpoint-kursk-region-143451765.html
Reuters - The governor of Russia's western Kursk region said several shells were fired on Saturday at a checkpoint near its border from the direction of Ukraine.
Speaking in a video posted on his Telegram channel, governor Roman Starovoit said that there were no casualties or damage.
