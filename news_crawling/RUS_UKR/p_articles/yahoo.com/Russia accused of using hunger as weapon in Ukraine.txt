Russia accused of using hunger as weapon in Ukraine
20220408
https://news.yahoo.com/russia-accused-using-hunger-weapon-231029656.html
Ukrainian President Zelenskyy is accusing Russia of using hunger as a weapon in cities under siege. 80-year-old Olga Dyomina tells NBC News Gabe Gutierrez that Russian soldiers blocked her town from food and supplies. Now organizations like World Central Kitchen are rushing in, delivering more than 135,000 meals a day.
