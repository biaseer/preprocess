Ukraine citizens charge a Russian vehicle
20220302
https://universe.byu.edu/2022/03/01/ukraine-citizens-charge-a-russian-vehicle/
Citizens in Kupyansk, Ukraine charged a Russian vehicle with yellow and blue flags held high. The unprovoked Russian attacks have Ukrainian citizens, as well as people all around the world, protesting against the violence. People are speaking up and fighting back as nations continue to isolate Russia.
