Biden to Kyiv Decisions on Ukraine only possible with Ukrainian involvement
20211210
https://bnn-news.com/biden-to-kyiv-decisions-on-ukraine-only-possible-with-ukrainian-involvement-230799
President Biden underscored the readiness of the United States to engage in support of confidence-building measures to advance the implementation of the Minsk Agreements, in support of the Normandy Format, American officials stated. Jen Psaki, spokeswoman for the US government, added that Biden briefed Zelensky on recent talks with the Russian President, as well as reaffirmed support for Ukraines sovereignty and territorial integrity, Ukrinform reports.
The conversation between Ukrainian and US presidents took place on Thursday, December 9. President Biden made clear that the United States and its allies and partners are committed to the principle of no decisions or discussions about Ukraine without Ukraine, according to a statement by the White House in Washington.

The leaders also agreed to call on Russia to de-escalate tensions and agreed that diplomacy is the best way to make meaningful progress on conflict resolution.
Due to Russia amassing its troops near its border with Ukraine and a conversation this week between Russian and American presidents, US President Joe Biden has had a call with his Ukrainian counterpart Volodymyr Zelensky, Ukrinform news agency reports.
