MOSCOW, August 25. TASS. More than 25,000 people have been evacuated from dangerous regions in Ukraine, and from the Lugansk and Donetsk Peoples Republics to Russia during the day, Mikhail Mizintsev, chief of Russias National Defense Management Center, said on Thursday.
The military official also said that over the past 24 hours, 22 appeals have been received with requests for evacuation to Russia, to the territory of the Donetsk and Lugansk Peoples Republics as well as to the areas of the Zaporozhye, Nikolaev, Kharkov and Kherson Regions liberated by Russias armed forces.
