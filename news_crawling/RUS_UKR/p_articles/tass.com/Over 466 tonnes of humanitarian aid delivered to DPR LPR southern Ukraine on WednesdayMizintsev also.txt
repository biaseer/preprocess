MOSCOW, August 17. TASS. Six humanitarian actions are underway on Wednesday in the Donetsk and Lugansk Peoples Republics as well as in the south of Ukraine, Mikhail Mizintsev, chief of Russias National Defense Management Center, reported.
According to Mizintsev, who also heads the Russian Joint Coordination Headquarters for Humanitarian Response in Ukraine, since March 2, a total of 56,421.9 tonnes of humanitarian cargoes have been delivered to Ukraine by Russia. As many as 1,464 humanitarian operations have been conducted.
"Six humanitarian operations are being conducted on August 17, 2022 in the Donetsk and Lugansk Peoples Republics, as well as in the Zaporozhye, Kharkov and Kherson regions, in which the population receives 466.7 tonnes of essentials and food," Mizintsev said.
