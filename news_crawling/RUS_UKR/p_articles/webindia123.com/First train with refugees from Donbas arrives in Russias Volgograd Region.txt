"Today, the first train with refugees from Donbas arrived in the Volgograd region. As many as 443 people are being delivered to temporary accommodation centres - the places that have been prepared in accordance with tasks set by the governor," the authorities said in a statement.Among the refugees, there are 166 children under the age of 14 including nine babies under one.The temporary accommodation centres have been organized in Volgograd hotels and a health centre, according to the statement.
