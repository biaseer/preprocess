US Assistant Secretary of State Donfried to visit Moscow Kyiv Washington
20211211

"Assistant Secretary for the Bureau of European and Eurasian Affairs, Karen Donfried will travel to Kyiv, Ukraine and Moscow, Russia December 13-15 to meet with senior government officials," a press statement said.After Russia and Ukraine, the US official will head to Brussels, where consultations with NATO allies and EU partners will take place. The talks will focus on the diplomatic resolution of the situation in Ukraine. ANISputnik
