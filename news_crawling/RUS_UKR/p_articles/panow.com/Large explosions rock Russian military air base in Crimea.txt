Large explosions rock Russian military air base in Crimea
20220710
https://panow.com/2022/08/09/large-explosions-rock-russian-military-air-base-in-crimea/
KYIV, Ukraine AP  Powerful explosions rocked a Russian air base in Crimea on Tuesday, and at least five people, including a child, were wounded, authorities said.
Russias Defense Ministry said that munitions blew up at the Saki base, and it emphasized that the installation had not been shelled. But Ukrainian social networks were abuzz with speculation that it was hit by Ukrainian-fired long-range missiles.
Videos posted on social networks showed sunbathers fleeing a nearby beach as huge clouds of smoke from the explosions rose over the horizon.
